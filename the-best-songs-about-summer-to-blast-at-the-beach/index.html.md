# The 9 Best Songs about Summer to Blast at the Beach ...

You're probably already pumped about the warm weather, but you should listen to music about summer in order to get even more excited about all the fun things you're going to do during the season. After all, there's no better time for you to party with your friends to create memories that you'll want to tell your children about when you're older. In honor of the best season of the year, here are some of the greatest songs about summer to blast at the beach:

## 1. All Summer Long by Kid Rock

Summer is meant for partying. It's the one time of year when you're encouraged to enjoy the moment without worrying about what the future has in store for you. Listen to Kid Rock's advice (except for the pieces about drugs) and go out to have an amazing time with your favorite people!

## 2. Soak up the Sun by Sheryl Crow

If you find sunbathing relaxing, you'll love the message in this song. It's all about stepping outside and enjoying nature instead of worrying about the problems in your life. Just enjoy what you have.

## 3. School’s out by Alice Cooper

The best thing about this season? No school, of course! If you hate waking up before the sun rises to run off to class, you'll love this song, especially once you get to the chorus.

## 4. California Girls by Katy Perry

You've definitely heard this song before. Even if you've never stepped foot in California, this is the perfect song to sing at the beach. It'll get you in the mood for fun!

## 5. Sufin’ USA by the Beach Boys

This is an old song, but it's still incredibly catchy. Whether you love to surf on waves, or just like splashing around in them, you'll appreciate these lyrics.

## 6. Summer Nights from Grease

If you haven't seen Grease yet, what are you doing with your life? It's one of the best musicals ever made, so make sure you look it up on Netflix. If you've never had an interest in watching it, you will after hearing this song about young love.

## 7. Summer Paradise by Simple Plan

You're probably used to Simple Plan's depressing songs from their younger days. However, their more current music is a bit more positive. This song is about a guy who had an amazing summer with a girl he's crazy about.

## 8. All Summer by Kid Cudi

If you're a rap fan, then this song will get you bobbing your head. Even if you don't like rap, you'll enjoy the song once the chorus begins. The video that goes along with it is a teensy bit strange, but that's what makes it so entertaining.

## 9. Summer Boy by Lady Gaga

You know Lady Gaga, but have you ever heard this song before? It's about a girl with a summer fling. If you've found a cutie you just want to have a little bit of harmless fun with, then this song will explain your situation perfectly.

Summer is the perfect time to have fun with your friends and family, so take advantage of the season. It's going to go by fast, so make sure you create some memories you can brag about a few months from now. What's your favorite song about this season?
