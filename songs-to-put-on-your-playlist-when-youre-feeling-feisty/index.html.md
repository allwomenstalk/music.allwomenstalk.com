# 7 Songs to Put on Your Playlist when Youre Feeling Feisty ...

Everybody has those days when they're in the mood for a fight. If you're not feeling all that upbeat, then music can be the cure you're looking for. All you have to do is turn on your radio, turn up the volume, and enjoy the sweet sounds. Here are some songs to put on your playlist when you're feeling feisty:

## 1. Just like You by Falling in Reverse

There's no reason to be upset by your mistakes, because everybody else screws up, too. Here's a song where the singer openly admits that he's not the nicest person on the planet, but he doesn't really care. Why not? Because he knows he's not alone. Everybody else is in the same boat, so why should he apologize for being himself?

## 2. Gunpowder and Lead by Miranda Lambert

If you aren't a fan of Miranda Lambert yet, that's about to change. This song gives a new definition to the phrase 'girl power.' Miranda shows that she's not one to be messed with. Even though she's a lady, she knows how to protect herself, so you should think twice before you do her wrong.

## 3. This is War by 30 Seconds to Mars

If you're not a fan of long intros, the lyrics begin 50 seconds into the video. It has a pretty serious message behind it, but it's also great to listen to when you're having a rough day. It'll remind you that only certain things in life are worth fighting for. It's up to you to make that distinction.

## 4. Sleazy by Kesha

Whether or not you love Kesha, you have to admit that she's created some pretty catchy songs. This one is all about how she doesn't need diamonds and fancy cars to be happy. She'd rather hang out in her basement than at a club where some of her friends are left out. This is great to listen to when you're fed up with everyone's obsession with money, because you know you don't need it to be happy.

## 5. Know Your Enemy by Green Day

When you're in a bad mood, it helps to sing about your enemies, which is why this song is perfect. You've probably heard it on the radio before, but it's time to add it to your iPod if you haven't already. I mean, you can never get enough Green Day.

## 6. Break Stuff by Limp Bizkit

This one has quite a few curses, so beware if you only like listening to clean music. However, it's great to listen to when you're having "one of those days." It'll help you release all of your anger, so you can go back to being the happy person you are.

## 7. Bodies by Drowning Pool

Here's one that's a bit creepy. Of course, the scariness is what makes it such a great song to listen to when you're having a rough day. Once you hear it once, you'll want to keep listening to it for the rest of the day, so make sure you have some time on your hands.

Music makes everything better, which is why you should never forget to pack your earbuds in your purse. You never know when you'll need a quick music break. What's your favorite song to listen to when you're feeling feisty?
