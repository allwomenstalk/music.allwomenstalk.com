# 7 Disney Songs That Will Make You Believe in Love ...

There are dozens of **Disney songs** that deserve high praise. Although the company has created a variety of movies, most of them have one thing in common: love. Love is such a big part of everyone's life, which is why we enjoy seeing it up on the big screen. Whether or not you consider yourself a romantic, here are some sweet Disney songs that will make you want to fall in love yourself:

## 1. Can You Feel the Love Tonight?

The Lion King contains some surprisingly emotional moments. When Simba and Nala reunite after years of being apart, their connection is still strong. Although silly characters like Timon and Pumbaa sing part of the lyrics, it's one of the Disney songs that accurately portrays true love. It's sweet, adorable, and incredibly catchy.

## 2. Kiss the Girl

The Little Mermaid shows the thought process of two people that are dying to kiss. Sometimes, it's hard to initiate the gesture, even when it's clear you're both interested. The song suggests that you have to stop being scared, and stop hiding your feelings. If you want to kiss someone, go for it!

## 3. A Whole New World

When you enter a new relationship, it's like you're entering an entirely new world. This song shows you how different your life will be if you let someone in, and fall in love. Change isn't always a negative thing. Sometimes, it's the best thing that can happen to you. Just ask Jasmine.

## 4. Some Day My Prince Will Come

This song from Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs inspires hope. If you're worried about being alone for the rest of your life, then give this a listen. Even if you feel like you're never going to find your prince, you have to believe in your heart that you'll meet someday. Hope is dangerous to lose, so keep your chin up!

## 5. How Does She Know?

Of course, not all Disney movies are animated. Even though this Enchanted makes fun of stereotypical fairytale relationships, it still portrays love in a positive light. If you're not sure how to tell whether someone truly loves you, listen to this song and it'll suddenly be clear.

## 6. Bella Notte

The lyrics to this song are absolutely adorable. It's sung in Lady and the Tramp, during the famous spaghetti and meatball kiss. Even though you're watching two pups fall in love, it still manages to be relatable. The awkwardness and tension they share is something we've all been through--or at least wish that we'll go through it in the future. If you're lucky, you'll even get that extra special kiss.

## 7. You’ll Be in My Heart

How long has it been since you've watched Tarzan? This song sounds more like it belongs on the radio than a Disney movie. It doesn't have the typical upbeat tune sung by the characters, but it's fantastic. You won't be able to listen to this only once. As soon as you play it, you won't be able to stop listening.

Even if you haven't seen all of these films, you can enjoy their message about love. They show that true love can survive anything, and that it's an important part of life. What is your favorite Disney movie of all time? Was it included on this list?
