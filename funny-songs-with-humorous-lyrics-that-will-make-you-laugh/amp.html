***

Bowling for Soup's "High School Never Ends" is an upbeat, fun song that speaks to people of all ages. It also uses humor to compare celebrities to stereotypical high school cliques, making it a great song to laugh along to.

Bowling for Soup is an American rock band that formed in 1994 in Wichita Falls, Texas. They are best known for their humorous lyrics and catchy melodies, and have released eight studio albums and one greatest hits album. The band has had several singles appear on the Billboard charts, including "1985," "Girl All the Bad Guys Want," and "High School Never Ends."

The song "High School Never Ends" was released in 2006 as the lead single from the album of the same name. It peaked at number 6 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, and was certified gold in the US and Canada. The song has been covered by several artists, including the Plain White T's and the Canadian band Walk off the Earth.
