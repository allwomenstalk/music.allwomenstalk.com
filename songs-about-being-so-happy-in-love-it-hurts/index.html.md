# 7 Songs about Being so Happy in Love It Hurts ...

Are you lucky enough to be dating the love of your life? If you are, it's probably hard to explain to your friends and family just how happy you are. If they can't understand your intense emotions, don't bother trying to explain it to them, because your favorite singers can do the talking for you. They capture the feeling of love perfectly in these songs about being so happy in love that it actually hurts:

## 1. You Make Me Smile by Uncle Kracker

You've probably heard this song before, because it was pretty popular a few years ago. It's about a man who knows just how lucky he is to have such a lovely girlfriend. She makes him happier than anything else in the world does, and he's proud to sing about it. If you and your man don't have a special song yet, this just might be the one to play on your wedding day.

## 2. James Dean and Audrey Hepburn by Sleeping with Sirens

When you find the perfect match, you'll wonder how you got to be so lucky. I mean, why would someone so amazing want to be with someone like you? Well, it doesn't matter, so long as you two are happy. If you're planning on being with your partner forever, you'll melt when you hear the lyrics to this song.

## 3. The Way by Clay Aiken

You haven't forgotten about Clay Aiken, have you? This song is about a guy who can't take his eyes off of the girl he's in love with. There's just something about her that drives him crazy. It's hard to explain love, but this song does a good job of it.

## 4. Marry Me by Jason Derulo

Do you dream about marrying your boyfriend? Then you'll love this song, because it's about knowing that you're with the person you're eventually going to marry. Even though now isn't the time to get engaged, you know in your heart that it'll happen eventually. You two are just meant to be.

## 5. Adore You by Miley Cyrus

Don't let Miley's crazy antics fool you--she's still an amazing singer. This song is about a women who absolutely adores her man. She loves him, and wishes that he would've entered her life sooner, because she never felt truly alive until she met him.

## 6. Pieces of Me by Ashlee Simpson

This is an old song, but you'll probably remember it. It's about a girl who has fallen for a guy that's made her life so much better. Even though he's seen all of the weird sides of her, he's still in love with her. This song will remind you that you should find a guy who you can be yourself around. There's no reason to pretend to be someone else, because the right guy will love every piece of you.

## 7. Rhythm of My Heart by Plain White T's

This song compares love to a beautiful song. It explains that you should be happy with your relationship today, even if you two don't stay together forever. Enjoy the love while it lasts--and if you're super lucky, it will never end.

Love is a magical emotion, so if you're fortunate enough to feel it, don't take it for granted. You're one of the lucky ones. What other songs do you know about being so happy in love that it hurts?
