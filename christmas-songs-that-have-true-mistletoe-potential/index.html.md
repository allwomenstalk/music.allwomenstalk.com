# 17 Christmas Songs That Have True Mistletoe Potential ...

You’re at a party. The drinks are flowing, the conversation is buzzing and you’re hoping to climb on board the seasonal love train. Make sure the mistletoe is close by, put on a romantic Christmas song and pucker up ready for a lip lock.

## 1. Baby, It’s Cold outside by Michael Bublé and Idina Menzel

This is perfect tune for the mistletoe; it encourages everybody to stay at the Christmas party and encourages them to snuggle up!

## 2. I Saw Mommy Kissing Santa Clause by the Jackson 5

The Jackson 5 version of this classic is really cheeky and fun. The perfect song to get people in the mood for doing some kissing of their own!

## 3. Let It Snow by Dean Martin

One of the all time Christmas classics, Dean Martin’s silky smooth vocals will definitely give you some success under the mistletoe!

## 4. Last Christmas by Wham!

The song is perfect because it’s about finding someone special after a heartbreak. What better time to start again than Christmas!?

## 5. Santa Baby by Eartha Kitt

This might be one of the most sensual and sexily cheeky Christmas songs of all time. It will get anybody in the mood for the mistletoe!

## 6. All I Want for Christmas is You by Mariah Carey

This song makes everyone so instantly happy and jolly that you will be guaranteed some mistletoe action when it comes on!

## 7. Winter Wonderland by Bing Crosby

The smooth listening of Big Crosby’s voice matched up with the beautiful and fun lyrics of this classic will get anyone in the mood for a festive smooch!

## 8. Christmas Eve by Celine Dion

This fun festive track by Celine Dion has all the right components to make it a sure fire winner in terms of your chances for a smooch under the mistletoe!

## 9. Merry Christmas, Darling by the Carpenters

The sweet, subtle voice of the late great Karen Carpenter makes this festive track super romantic and super perfect for mistletoe scenes.

## 10. I’ll Be Home for Christmas by Michael Bublé

The dulcet tones of the amazing Michael Bublé are enough to make anyone amorous, and that is certainly the case with lovely long song about reaching your loved one for the Holidays.

## 11. This Christmas by Donny Hathaway

Donny Hathaway adds a touch of soul to Christmas with this amazing song. Who could resist a quick kiss with this playing?

## 12. Underneath the Tree by Kelly Clarkson

This modern classic by Kelly Clarkson is so catchy and infectious that it will get everybody in the mood for festive mistletoe and romance!

## 13. Winter Song by Sara Bareilles and Ingrid Michaelson

This is a little different to the usual Christmas song, but the hypnotic tone of both singers’ voices creates an amazing atmosphere of love.

## 14. My Only Wish (This Year) by Britney Spears

This infectious pop song by the Princess of Pop herself will definitely get you in the mood for some cheeky festive smooching!

## 15. Mistletoe by Justin Bieber

Whether you love him or hate him, you can’t deny that this Christmas song by a young Bieber does the trick as a soundtrack to a festive mistletoe kiss!

## 16. One More Sleep by Leona Lewis

Leona Lewis released an instant Christmas classic with this one, all about wanting to be with the one you love at Christmas. If you didn’t have somebody before the song started, you’ll definitely have one by the time it’s finished!

## 17. Snowflakes of Love by Toni Braxton

The amazingly soulful voice of Toni Braxton makes for creating the perfect romantic atmosphere to be able to kiss your crush underneath the mistletoe.

Which is your favorite romantic Christmas song? Will you be kissing along to it under the mistletoe this year or hoping for a new love to enter your life in 2018?
