Easy Listening Playlist to Soothe Your Soul ...

"Fly Away" by Alison Krauss • "I Need You" by America • "Don’t Worry" by the Beach Boys • "All I Can do" by the Cars • "in My Place" by Coldplay • More ...

By Emily

There are some days when you just need to sit down and listen to some music that is easy on the ears. That's when you need an easy listening playlist. Nothing to pump you or to make you dance, but soft tunes to de-stress after a busy day. I have put together a suggested playlist of songs that I find relaxing and enjoy listening to when I’m not in the usual mood of amped-up rock or pop songs. The list is in alphabetical order by author, as it is hard to rate these songs from best to worse. Some of these artists have multiple songs that would fit the list but I only chose one song from each artist to avoid repetition. I highly encourage you to look these artists’ music for the ultimate easy listening playlist.

1. "Fly Away" by Alison Krauss

2. "I Need You" by America

3. "Don’t Worry" by the Beach Boys

4. "All I Can do" by the Cars

5. "in My Place" by Coldplay

6. "Put Your Records on" by Corinne Bailey Rae

7. "Sweet Judy Blue Eyes" by Crosby, Stills, and Nash

8. "What I Am" by Edie Brickell

9. "Lay down Sally" by Eric Clapton

10. "Dreams" by Fleetwood Mac

11. "Here Comes the Sun" by George Harrison (the Beatles)

12. "Truckin’" by Grateful Dead

13. "Cruisin’" by Huey Lewis Ft. Gwyneth Paltrow

14. "Somewhere over the Rainbow" by Israel Kamakawiwo'ole

15. "Free Fallin’" by John Mayer

16. "Sunshine" by Jonathan Edwards

17. "Wash Away" by Joe Purdy

18. "Smile" by Lily Allen

19. "Ain’t No Mountain High Enough" by Marvin Gaye

20. "Where I Go" by Natalie Merchant

21. "Dreamer’s Ball" by Queen

22. "Scar Tissue" by Red Hot Chili Peppers

23. "There She Goes" by Sixpence None the Richer

24. "do It Again" by Steely Dan

25. "Stumblin'" in by Suzi Quatro

26. "Hold Me Now" by Thompson Twins

27. "Hello It’s Me" by Todd Rundgren

Please tell me what songs are on your playlist in the comments. I hope you decide to add some of these songs to your music.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is 'Easy Listening' music?
Oh, it's that super chill, laid-back kind of music that you can just vibe to. Think of it as the soundtrack for a relaxing day at the spa or a cozy evening at home. It's perfect for unwinding.
Why do people love easy listening playlists?
Well, because they're like a warm hug! They help calm the mind, reduce stress, and make you feel all sorts of cozy and at ease. Plus, they're great for background music when you’re working or studying!
Can easy listening music help with my anxiety?
Totally! Many folks find that these soothing tunes can help ease anxiety and create a peaceful atmosphere. So next time you're feeling a bit tense, maybe give it a try!
How do I start creating my own easy listening playlist?
Super simple! Start with songs that make you feel relaxed and happy. You can look up some popular playlists on streaming platforms for inspiration, or just go with your gut and pick tunes that give you all the good vibes!
Are there any all-time favorite easy listening songs you recommend?
Oh, there are tons! Think classics from artists like Frank Sinatra or more recent soothing hits. Everyone has their faves, but a little trip to YouTube or Spotify could uncover some real gems for you!
