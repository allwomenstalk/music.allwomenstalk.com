# 7 Country Songs for Fall That Need to Be on Your Playlist ...

Most people typically associate great country songs with summertime, but there are so many incredible country songs for fall that need to be on your playlist ASAP. Country music tells a story that few other genres can do with such artistry, not to mention it welcomes in fall like the changing of the leaves. Whether it be upbeat or slow, so many country songs for fall are perfect for snuggling up or dancing around all season.

## 1. Don’t Ya by Brett Eldredge

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This song has been stuck in my head for the past few weeks, which is why it is first on my list of perfect country songs for fall. It isn’t enough that Brett Eldredge has the most delicious voice ever, but this song is just so darn catchy. I dare you to listen to it on your way to class and not walk in step to it.

## 2. Redneck Crazy by Tyler Farr

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Nothing gets me more than a country breakup song. Tyler Farr is relatively new on the country music scene and this song is guaranteed to make a lasting impression. Farr’s voice wraps around you like your favorite cozy fall sweater.

## 3. Parking Lot Party by Lee Brice

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This country song is guaranteed to get you in the fall party mood. It is cheerful and upbeat like the typical summer anthem, but it reminds me of the perfect fall tailgate. Who knows, maybe I’ll start calling football tailgates parking lot parties instead?

## 4. Hey Pretty Girl by Kip Moore

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While most of my favorite country songs are upbeat, this is a lot mellower and calming. The lyrics of this song are so simple yet so moving. Add in Kip Moore’s incredible voice and there is no way this song shouldn’t be on your country songs for fall playlist.

## 5. Kiss Tomorrow Goodbye by Luke Bryan

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Why should all of the fall songs be about love? I am a sucker for anything by Luke Bryan, and this breakup song is no exception. What makes it so perfect is that it is not the typical breakup song. It realizes that the relationship is over and moves on in a not-so typical way. I guess you have to listen to the lyrics to figure out what that means.

## 6. Come over by Kenny Chesney

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The perfect fall songs for me are calming and that is the perfect way to describe “Come Over” by Kenny Chesney. His deep voice is so smooth to begin with. Combine that with simple instruments like guitar and drums and you have the perfect fall song.

## 7. Colder Weather by Zac Brown Band

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This song isn’t exactly new, but what better way to bring in some nice cold weather with a song called “Colder Weather”? Its use of violin is perfect to help you focus for your dreaded midterms, not to mention that Zac Brown’s voice is absolutely wonderful. This song is definitely not one to skip on your country fall playlist.

No longer is country only reserved for summertime. The beauty about country music is that you can listen to it all year long and still appreciate it.
