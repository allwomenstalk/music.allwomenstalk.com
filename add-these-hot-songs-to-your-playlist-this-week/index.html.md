# 17 Songs on My Playlist This Week That Should Be on Yours ...

Sometimes you get tired of listening to the same old songs, but finding new music is hard unless you're always listening to the radio or going rogue on Pandora. Here are the songs pumping up my playlist this week. Feel free to share your recommendations, too!

## 1. Body like a Back Road by Sam Hunt

This song is great. I'm a sucker for a good country song and this definitely tops my charts.

## 2. Can't Stop the Feeling by Justin Timberlake

JT has definitely taken his career to another level after going solo many moons ago. I'm a mom, so this definitely is in my head all day from having Trolls on repeat. And I can't help but dance anytime it comes on!

## 3. Closer by the Chainsmokers

This song has been out awhile, but it's still so popular it remains in Billboards Top 100 in the top 30. It's so catchy and I love to jam to this song when I'm working out.

## 4. Shape of You by Ed Sheeran

This song has such a funky beat. It's so easy to get your groove on to! Plus, you can never go wrong with Ed Sheeran.

## 5. I Don't Wanna Live Forever by Zayn Feat. Taylor Swift

OMG this song! I've been in love since the Fifty Shades Darker trailer released. It's just so good I can't describe it!

## 6. We Don't Talk Anymore by Charlie Puth Feat. Selena Gomez

I absolutely love the tempo of this song. It's funky, upbeat, and definitely is on my must listen to list lately.

## 7. Treat You Better by Sean Mendez

I'm all about the lyrics when it comes to most songs, so this one is great. It's sweet and easy to listen to and I totally don't mind it being stuck in my head all day.

## 8. Heathens by Twenty-one Pilots

Another song that's been out for a minute but continues to hold popularity. It's hands down a great song and I can't even begin to describe why.

## 9. Don't Let Me down by the Chainsmokers Feat. Daya

I need this song on my playlist right now and probably for awhile. It's a great song when I hop on the treadmill that keeps me motivated.

## 10. Dirt on My Boots by Jon Pardi

Like I said, I love a good country song, and this is definitely one of them. From the lyrics to the beat, to Jon Pardi's sexy southern accent when he sings. I'm swooning every time this comes on the radio and my playlist.

## 11. God, Your Mama, and Me by Florida Georgia Line Feat Backstreet Boys

As if I didn't have enough reasons to love Florida Georgia Line, they give me this collab with BSB and I'm thrown back to the 90s! No words describe how fab this song is and how absolutely obsessed I am with it!

## 12. We Should Be Friends by Miranda Lambert

After spending the last almost decade in the south, I love this. Miranda Lambert is adorable in the video and the lyrics couldn't ring more true. If you don't have this on your playlist you need to ASAP!

## 13. Outskirts of Heaven by Craig Campbell

Nothing describes this song better than telling you to listen and you'll understand why it belongs on your playlist. Brings back so many memories of when my grandpa was alive and puts a smile on my face every time I hear it.

## 14. Road Less Traveled by Lauren Alaina

Lauren Alaina is my spirit animal with this song! Those lyrics are my soul it's ridiculous. And let's get real, this girl has swept the world by storm since she hit the scene. This song is my go-to running song because it's phenomenal and pumps me up.

## 15. How Not to by Dan + Shay

Everything Dan + Shay produces is pure gold and this is no different. A sweet love song with a much deeper meaning. Add this to your playlist and fall in love like I have.

## 16. Bounce Back by Big Sean

I swear my music taste is more diverse than my taste in food. This song is is great, especially when adding to your workout playlist. It comes on and you can't help but immediately start dancing... Or... Maybe that's just me. Haha!

## 17. Bad Things by Machine Gun Kelly and Camila Cabello

Call me crazy but Machine Gun Kelly is gold! From his tats to his beats this song definitely needs to be on my playlist! Plus, this girl's voice in this is crazy good! My car/shower jam will never compare but that won't stop me from belting out this song. Haha!

So, what songs are you listening to this week?
