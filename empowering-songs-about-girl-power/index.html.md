# 7 Empowering Songs about Girl Power ...

Us women have to stick together, which is why you should load your iPod up with empowering songs about girl power. The right music can change your attitude and make you see just how strong you are. That's why you shouldn't hesitate to get up to dance and flaunt your womanhood. Here are some of the most empowering, and catchy, songs about girl power that you'll want to listen to again and again:

## 1. Salute by Little Mix

You won't find anything with a better beat than this! The song is all about women knowing that they need to stick together. They're more than just pretty faces--they're capable human beings that can conquer the world. Even if you don't love the lyrics, you'll love the dance moves in the music video.

## 2. Run the World by Beyonce

You can't discuss girl power without mentioning Beyonce. She has plenty of empowering songs, like Single Ladies, but this one is by far the best. It's all about women being the real ones in charge of the world. Men might believe that they're the ones with control, but that's only because we let them think that way.

## 3. Girls by Jennifer Lopez

After the very first line of the song, you'll see why this song is empowering. It's about a girl who makes her own money and knows how to take care of herself. However, that doesn't mean that she can't go out and have fun. That's the point of life, after all. You might as well enjoy yourself.

## 4. Secrets by Mary Lambert

This song encourages women to stop worrying about their insecurities. There's no reason to pretend to be someone you're not, because you're amazing just the way you are. It's time to stop hiding your secrets and embrace who you are! Nothing bad is going to happen if you do. In fact, you'll feel much better about yourself once you start opening up.

## 5. Pretty Girl Rock by Keri Hilson

There's nothing wrong with flaunting your hotness. You shouldn't be shy about your body and constantly worry about what nasty things others could be thinking about you. If you know you're gorgeous, it's okay to be proud of it. You won't look cocky. You'll just look confident.

## 6. Fly by Nicki Minaj and Rihanna

You've probably heard this song before, because it's been on the radio more than a few times. It's about women who aren't afraid to soar. They know that they have what it takes to prosper, and they won't stop until they do. Nicki and Rihanna are the essence of girl power, so it's hard not to love this song.

## 7. This One’s for the Girls by Martina McBride

This song is great to listen to at any age, because Martina McBride addresses women of every age group. No matter how old you are, it'll reassure you that life is going to get better. Everything will turn out okay in the end, so don't worry too much about what's going to happen to you. You're strong enough to make it through anything.

Be proud that you're a woman! We live in an amazing time where we're free to go after any job we please, so take advantage of your opportunities. What other empowering songs about girl power have you heard?
