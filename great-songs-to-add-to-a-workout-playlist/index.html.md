# Great Songs to Add to a Workout Playlist for Killer Motivation Part 1 of 5 ...

Do you need some **great songs to add to a workout playlist**?
The best way to get motivated for exercise (or anything really) is with music, the most wonderful thing in the world. The right songs can get you pumped up to get moving, to break a sweat, and to hopefully burn some fat and calories. The right songs can change between genres; from rock to hip-hop. This list contains songs that I enjoy and that are great for working out. The playlist is split into five separate articles as the number of songs grew extensive: over 50. So here are the first set of **great songs to add to a workout playlist**.
