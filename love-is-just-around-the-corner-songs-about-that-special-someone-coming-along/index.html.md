# Love is Just around the Corner! Songs about That Special Someone Coming along ...

Waiting for your Prince Charming to come along? Well, if you're having trouble finding him, have no fear, because he's out there somewhere. He's just not ready to show his face just yet. If you don't believe that, here are a few songs about love being just around the corner that'll change your mind:

## 1. Rainbow by Colbie Caillat

This song is about waiting for love. Colbie Caillat wonders where her perfect man is and when she will finally meet him. See, even superstars have trouble in the romance department! So if you're hoping for a romance like Ross and Rachel's, it'll only be a matter of time until love blossoms. Just keep your head up until it does.

## 2. Just Haven’t Met You Yet by Michael Buble

Have you had your heart broken one too many times in the past? Well, you shouldn't give up on love, just because your exes weren't right for you. Why should you stay optimistic in such a hopeless world? Because your perfect partner is out there, and you just haven't met him yet--but you will one day.

## 3. Someone Somewhere by Jason Reeves

There's someone out there for you. Even though you may feel like you're alone in the world, there are plenty of guys out there who feel the same way. Eventually, you'll run into one of them and your love story will begin.

## 4. Love Song for No One by John Mayer

This song is about a man who is tired of being alone. He just wants his soulmate to hurry up and meet him already. Even though he's fed up with waiting, he continues to search for his perfect woman every single day. The only thing that frightens him is that he's already met her and missed his chance with her. This song will really get you thinking about every person you pass on the street.

## 5. Somewhere out There from an American Tail

This song isn't meant to be romantic, but you can interpret it as such. If the adorable lyrics don't get you emotional, then the sweet little mouse singing should do the trick. If you haven't seen this movie, then you're missing out, so make sure you head to the library to borrow it.

## 6. Goodnight My Someone from the Music Man

Here's another song from a film, and it's perfect to sing when you're daydreaming about the future love of your life. You don't need to know someone in order to be crazy about them, which is why you should sing along to this whenever you're feeling lonely. One day, you'll be able to do a duet with your partner.

## 7. Jane Doe by Never Shout Never

Jane Doe is a name used when a person's true identity is unknown. That's why it's a perfect nickname for your future lover, and why it's used so often in this song. The lyrics talk about a man wanting to know the name of his crush. Even though he doesn't know exactly who she is, he knows that she's absolutely everything that he's ever wanted.

Never give up on finding the man of your dreams, because he could be right around the corner. You don't want to miss out on the greatest relationship of your life, because you were too worried about what could go wrong, would you? What other songs do you listen to about waiting to find love?
