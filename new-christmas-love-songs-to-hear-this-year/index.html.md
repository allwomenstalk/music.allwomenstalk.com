# New Christmas Love Songs to Hear This Year ...

There’s nothing that puts us in the Christmas spirit quite like the music that comes with the season. There’s tons of classic songs that we all love ❤️ listening to, and what’s great is that our favorite singers always put out new versions of them. Whether you like country or pop or something in between, there is something for everyone to listen to. These are some of our favorite new love songs (or new covers of classics) to listen to this year.‌﻿‌🎅 ‌﻿‌

## 1. Celebrate Me Home by Leann Rimes Ft. Gavin DeGraw

The pairing of Rimes with DeGraw is so nice for this romantic and smooth Christmas jam. We love this version since their voices mesh so well together.

## 2. Santa Baby by Clare Bowen

Country music star Clare Bowen brings a great twang to this classic song that we simply adore.

## 3. Christmas (Baby Please Come Home) by Chris Young

We are in love with the new holiday album that Chris Young has put out, and he really delivers on every track.

## 4. I’ll Be Home for Christmas by Pentatonix

Pentatonix is blowing up right now and they’re amazing. This acapella version of a classic is a great new addition to your playlist.

## 5. What Are You Doing New Year’s Eve? by Kacey Musgraves

Kacey Musgraves has a great voice that goes beyond country and works well in the pop scene. She sounds beautiful doing this festive love song.

## 6. Only You by Kylie Minogue

We love this song by Kylie Minogue (featuring James Corden, who also sounds amazing). It’s a great new one to add to your playlist.

## 7. Mistletoe by Colbie Caillat

Colbie Caillat’s beachy vibes come through this song and make it laid back and relaxing.

## 8. Warm in December by Seth Macfarlane

Macfarlane’s voice compliments the title of this song well: it’s warm and embracing.

## 9. Love is Everything by Ariana Grande

Pop sensation Ariana Grande sings a great song about the reason for the season.

## 10. This Christmas by the Braxtons

The Braxtons change up the classic by including the line “This Christmas will be a Braxton family Christmas for me.”

## 11. I’ve Got My Love to Keep Me Warm by India Arie and Joe Sample

We love this blend of voices. This jazzy Christmas song is perfect and the lyrics are great as well.

## 12. Wrapped in Red by Kelly Clarkson

We just love Kelly Clarkson. Her voice is amazing and it sounds great on a Christmas album. The music video for this is adorable tooo.

## 13. Mr Right by Leona Lewis

Another powerhouse singer Leona Lewis brings a jolly and fun track that is perfect to sing along to.

## 14. Please Come Home for Christmas by Sharon Jones and the Dap Kings

We love this soulful song and funky vibes that exude from it.

## 15. What Are You Doing New Years Eve? by Idina Menzel

Okay, so we already have this song on here, but Broadway star Idina Menzel brings a whole different style to it, so both are great additions.

## 16. White Christmas by Michael Buble

A Christmas playlist would not be complete without Michael Buble… it would just feel wrong. He embodies Christmas with his crooning voice.

## 17. All I Want for Christmas is You by Lady Antebellum

We love the blend of voices in Lady Antebellum and they bring beautiful harmonies to the classic song.

## 18. Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas by Jordan Smith

Jordan Smith is here with a beautiful Christmas album after winning the 9th season of The Voice. It’s pretty amazing.

## 19. Someday at Christmas by Stevie Wonder and Andra Day

Two epic singers come together for a great Christmas track that you need to listen to, asap.

These are just a handful of the Christmas songs 🎄 that you should definitely give a listen to this season. Check them out and let us know what your picks are.
