# 7 Amazing John Mayer Songs Ill Never Get Tired of ...

There are so many John Mayer songs that I could listen to over and over again without getting tired of them. I mean, the man is a lyrical genius. I'm so bummed that I didn't go to his most recent tour with Phillip Phillips. I bet it was amazing. If you had the pleasure of going, chances are I'm 100% jealous of you! Here are the seven John Mayer songs that I can listen to time and time again.

## 1. Your Body is a Wonderland

I mean, must I explain why this is one of my favorite John Mayer songs? It's perfection from start to finish. It's just one of those feel-good songs that you wouldn't mind listening to day in and day out. The music video is even perfect. I dare you not to listen to this song and have it stuck in your head for the rest of the day.

## 2. Waiting on the World to Change

I absolutely LOVE this song! Countless people have covered this song but John's version has always been my favorite. The lyrics are beautiful and so is the music video. It'll never get old!

## 3. Heartbreak Warfare

When I first heard this song, I'm pretty sure I had it on repeat for the rest of the day. The lyrics are everything. I'm not exactly sure what movie I remember this song from, but I'm so happy I was watching that movie when I did. I was introduced to one of my favorite John Mayer songs.

## 4. Who You Love (ft. Katy Perry)

This is one of John's newest songs but it's so beautiful. It features his love, Katy Perry. They sound incredible together! Fingers crossed that they continue to work together and come out with more music. That would be amazing! I love the lyrics in this song!

## 5. Half of My Heart

This song is amazing. I know, it seems like I say that about every song from John. But really, everything he does is amazing. I love the lyrics and the melody to this song. It's just so catchy!

## 6. Daughters

This song is definitely a crowd favorite. I'll just never get tired of the beautiful lyrics. That man sure knows how to write beautiful music, huh? Lyrical genius, I tell you! It's such a cute family-oriented song. I admire John for writing about things like this, things that truly matter.

## 7. Clarity

This is one of John's older songs. It's definitely an oldie, but a goodie. It's such a feel-good song and has such a mellow vibe to it. One of my favorite covers of this song is by Cameron Mitchell (contestant from season one of The Glee Project). If you haven't heard it, definitely check it out on YouTube. It's not like the original, but it's definitely worth listening to. I love that John's music is covered by so many artists.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of MANY John Mayer songs that I'll never get tired of. Narrowing down the list to just seven was pretty tough so please don't think I'm not aware of the other genius songs from John! His newest album is full of fantastic music, but these are just some classics. Which song is your all-time favorite from John?
