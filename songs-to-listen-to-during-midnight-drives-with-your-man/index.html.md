# 7 Lovely Songs to Share with Your Man on a Late Night Drive ...

We all want a boyfriend who will pick us up in the middle of the night and drive us around while blasting music into the quiet streets. If you're lucky enough to have a man like that, or at least friends like that, then you need to choose the right soundtrack. It'll make the experience more fun than ever before. Here are a few songs to listen to during midnight drives with your man:

## 1. Perfect by One Direction

You're either a self-proclaimed super fan of One Direction or you're a liar. I mean, how could you resist a group of British boys who sing songs we can all relate to? This song in particular literally talks about "midnight driving with the windows down" and "going places we can't even pronounce." Now, doesn't that sound romantic?

## 2. White Walls by Macklemore and Ryan Lewis

You've listened to Macklemore and Ryan Lewis before, but you might not have heard this song yet. It's about "that off-black Cadillac, midnight drive." It's a catchy tune, so you're going to want to listen to it more than once. If you haven't been on a midnight drive anytime recently, then this song is going to inspire you to change that.

## 3. Hurricane by Halsey

If you don't know about Halsey yet, then it's time for that to change. This song isn't about an actual drive, but it's still great to listen to in the car with your man, because it's about having the time of your life with the people you love the most. It's great to listen to while reminiscing about old memories or while creating new memories.

## 4. 22 by Taylor Swift

It doesn't matter if you're already out of your 20s or if you haven't entered them yet. Either way, you're going to be moving and grooving to this song by the fabulous Taylor Swift. You've probably heard it before, but it's time for you to hear it again now that summer's approaching. It's the perfect road trip tune.

## 5. Windows down by Big Time Rush

Listen to this song the next time you want to drive down to the beach with your boyfriend in the middle of the night. It doesn't matter how dark out it is, because this song is going to give you enough energy to make it into the morning. It'll get your heart pumping.

## 6. Born to Be Wild by Steppenwolf

We can't forget about the classics. If you're going on a fun road trip with your man, even if it's only down to the closest McDonald's, then this is one of the songs you should have playing. It'll make you feel like a total badass, even if you're driving in a minivan instead of on a motorcycle.

## 7. Ocean Avenue by Yellowcard

This is one of Yellowcard's biggest hits, and it was famous for a reason. It's perfect to listen to when you're young and ready to go out on a big adventure. If you're planning on staying up all night, then you should play this song to keep you awake.

Now you have plenty of new songs to listen to during midnight drives with your man. What's your favorite song to listen to while you're cruising around town?
