# 7 Reasons to Love Lorde and Get on Her Team ...

If you aren't already obsessed with this new superstar, there are plenty of reasons to love Lorde. More and more people are joining her team each day. She even reached one million Twitter followers not too long ago! Let's face it, this girl is more successful at age 17 than we ever were. Who else can say they've accomplished so much so quickly? It truly is impressive. Check out the reasons to love Lorde and let me know what you think of this New Zealand artist.

## 1. You Just Don't Get Tired of "royals"

![You Just Don't Get Tired of "royals"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/mg/dt/bl5ie7kf_495x370.jpg)

You've probably heard her song, "Royals" countless times on the radio by now, yet, you still manage to jam out each time you hear it. That's the power of a good song; it sounds brand new each time you hear it. Sure, there are people who are tired of hearing it already, but they don't matter! This girl gets so much radio play because people are still requesting her music. That's just one of the reasons to love Lorde.

## 2. She Has Awesome Hair

![She Has Awesome Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/h8/c5/m0xtmgmy_600x450.jpg)

I mean, really. Lorde's hair is pretty epic. She has the most beautiful curls and she's not afraid to show them off. If you've watched her Royals music video, then you know firsthand how awesome those curls are. I definitely wish I had curly hair like her!

## 3. She's Only 17

![She's Only 17](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wx/n9/0o/0vgm0132_600x400.jpg)

It's mind-blowing how much she's achieved in such a short amount of time. She's 17 years old, held the top spot on Billboard's Hot 100, and went home with two Grammys last month. That's definitely more than I can say for my accomplishments at 17. How about you?

## 4. Amazing Stage Presence

![Amazing Stage Presence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/0z/et/bcanwzto_600x400.jpg)

Lorde is one of those performers that you just can't look away from. From start to finish, you're hooked on every word she's singing. I have a feeling that she'll be performing in stadiums very, very soon. Her US tours have sold out within hours of being on sale! That's crazy.

## 5. Major Celeb Endorsements

![Major Celeb Endorsements](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ry/z6/l9/napp17ct_600x373.jpg)

It seems like everyone who's anyone has been obsessed with Lorde. Pretty Little Liars' Ashley Benson gushed about Lorde's performance at the Pre-Grammys party and Vanessa Hudgens even said she'd recommend Lorde as a music suggestion for her friends, on Late Night With Jimmy Fallon. When Lorde's album, Pure Heroine, dropped, celebs gave it rave reviews. Looks like Hollywood is already on Lorde's team.

## 6. She Speaks Her Mind through Lyrics

![She Speaks Her Mind through Lyrics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/vi/iu/uhrhjcvh_600x400.jpg)

I think it's really refreshing how Lorde uses her music to speak her mind. Her lyrics include things like, "don't you think that it's boring how people talk?", and "we crave a different kind of buzz." Her lyrics are really mature. Who's even that self-aware at age 17? Only Lorde.

## 7. Talented

![Talented](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/0n/te/yvlqytgy_410x304.jpg)

Another refreshing thing about Lorde is how talented she actually is. She writes her own music, and works so incredibly hard on perfecting her craft. I also love that Lorde doesn't feel the need to be half naked in her music videos. Her music alone grabs our attention and that's all she needs.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many reasons why you should be listening to and loving Lorde. She's definitely in it for the long haul; she'll be taking over the music biz really, really soon! What's your favorite thing about her?
