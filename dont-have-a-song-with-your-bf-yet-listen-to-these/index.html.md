# Dont Have a Song with Your BF Yet? Listen to These!

Music can be a huge part of any relationship. If you and your significant other love music, having a “song” is probably very important to both of you. If you can’t find a song that perfectly sums up your relationship, I’ve got you covered. No matter the kind of relationship you have with your favorite guy, I’m sure that you’ll find a song here that reminds you of each other, whether you're together or apart, every single time you hear it!

## 1. You Are in Love by Taylor Swift

I am in love with this song. Seriously, if you haven’t heard it, give it a listen. It has a little bit of a Bruce Springsteen vibe to it that makes it feel so timeless. She perfectly captures the feeling of falling in love, which is what everyone wants when they’re looking for a “song” to capture the essence of their relationship!

## 2. Wildfire by Ben Rector

I am a huge Ben Rector fan, so I think that all of his songs would make good songs for you and your boyfriend, but this is one especially great. Do you feel like your relationship just lit your whole life on fire (in the best way possible, of course!)? If so, then this is the song for you and your significant other!

## 3. I like You by Ben Rector

If you’re not into the frills that come along with a lot of love songs, this is the song for you and your boyfriend. Ben Rector does love songs right, and this song is no exception. (I told you that he’s my favorite, right?) It’s straight, to the point, and happy enough that you’ll want to keep listening to it for years with your significant other!

## 4. Electric Love by BØRNS

Are you and your significant other into pop songs rather than acoustic love songs? Then Electric Love is for you! This fun and upbeat song will always make you want to get up and dance with your person whenever you hear it! It’s still a love song, but it’s a fun love song that will make you want to dance around the house!

## 5. My My Love by Joshua Radin

Something about this song reminds me of a quiet, rainy day spent with the person you love. If you and your significant other aren’t big into dramatics and are pretty low-key, this could definitely be the song to capture your relationship!

## 6. You and I by Ingrid Michaelson

This is one of my all-time favorite songs, simply because it’s so catchy and upbeat. Whether you’re looking for a song to listen to with your favorite guy or want something to dance to at your wedding, this song has got you covered no matter the occasion!

## 7. Forever by Ben Harper

If you’re in it for the long haul with your significant other, this song will perfectly sum that up. It’s called Forever, and that’s exactly what this song is about-a forever relationship. If you feel that way about your relationship, this is the song for you!

What are you favorite songs for when you’re in a relationship? Will any of these become your “song” with your boyfriend? Let me know which of these songs is your favorite in the comments!
