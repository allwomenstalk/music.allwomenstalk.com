# 7 Songs That Will Boost Your Confidence ...

When you're feeling bad about yourself, there are songs that will boost your confidence. Music is medicine for the soul. It never fails to bring a smile to the saddest person's face. The next time you have a rough day, listen to these songs that will boost your confidence.

## 1. Everybody Loves Me by OneRepublic

There's nothing wrong with being a little cocky every once in a while. This song is all about bragging: "Feels just like I don't try. Look so good, I might die. All I know is everybody loves me." Even if you don't feel like you have your life put together, singing these lyrics will temporarily make you feel like you're the Queen of the World. This is one of the songs that will boost your confidence, because it allows you a few minutes to feel like you're number one.

## 2. Just the Way You Are by Bruno Mars

Whether you're in a loving relationship or single, you at least have one man that will compliment you. Bruno sings about how gorgeous his girl is, even though she has low confidence. "Her laugh, she hates, but I think it's so sexy." He's reminding every girl that even their 'flaws' are lovable. Once you find the right guy, he'll love everything about you.

## 3. One in a Million by the Backstreet Boys

There's nothing wrong with listening to old songs. This is another one about a girl who doesn't realize how amazing she is. She goes through her life without realizing that she has admirers that love everything about her. "She screams on the pain. I hear every word. Why don't you know how beautiful you are?"

## 4. What Makes You Beautiful by One Direction

Let's stay with the boyband theme for a second. Even if you're not crazy about these Brits, you have to admit that their songs are catchy. You've probably heard this song before, so it won't be new to you. I'm sure you can all sing these lyrics in your head in the correct tune: "Everyone else in the room can see it. Everyone else but you." However, it's something that you can listen to again and again.

## 5. Beautiful by Christina Aguilera

Christina proves that even the most gorgeous women suffer from insecurity. There are days when she feels like she isn't pretty enough, but she rises above her confidence issues. "You are beautiful, no matter what they say. Words can't bring you down." This song is inspirational, and can make even the saddest girl crack a smile.

## 6. Don't You Know You're Beautiful by Kelly Pickler

Kelly talks about the issues girls have at every age of their life. It doesn't matter how old someone is, they'll always have insecurities. However, Kelly believes that "Your worth ain't on a price tag, it comes from within." She knows that beauty has nothing to do with a girl's clothing or hair. It's all about how they act and how they feel.

## 7. Perfect by Pink

Pink talks about the hatred every girl has for herself. No matter what you accomplish in life, you'll always believe that you're not good enough. However, you have to ignore the voice that tells you that you're less than perfect: "You're so mean when you talk about yourself, you were wrong. Change the voices in your head. Make them like you instead." You're amazing, and you should always remember that.

Music contains the power to heal. Whenever you feel like you're not good enough, listen to one of these songs, or even put them on a playlist. Who do you listen to when you're in a crummy mood?
