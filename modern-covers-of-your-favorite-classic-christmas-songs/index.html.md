# 7 Modern Covers of Your Favorite Classic Christmas Songs ...

There are some classic Christmas songs that have been around for decades. Even though you've heard them a million times, you can't get bored of them, because there are always new covers being made. So if you're sick of the old songs, here are a few modern covers of your favorite Christmas songs:

## 1. Baby, It's Cold outside by Lady Antebellum

It doesn't matter if you love country or not, because either way, you'll enjoy this song. You should already know the concept of it. It's about a man trying to convince a woman to stay the night, because it's too cold outside for her to leave. Of course, he really just wants to get close to her--and she wants to get close to him, too.

## 2. Last Christmas by Taylor Swift

Taylor has sung quite a few Christmas songs. However, this is the best one she's covered. After all, we're used to her singing songs about boys that have broken her heart. That's why this song is the perfect fit for her.

## 3. Santa Baby by Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is getting more famous by the day. She has a unique voice, which is perfect for singing a song like this. She manages to strike the perfect balance between cute and sexy. Of course, Liz Gillies also sings this song along with her. Even if you've never heard of her before, this song will make it clear that the two make an amazing pair.

## 4. Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas by Michael Buble

Michael Buble loves Christmas. Every year, you can find him performing during holiday shows, like the Rockefeller Christmas tree lighting. He even has an entire Christmas album out. That means he's used to singing classic songs--and his voice is perfect for it. It sounds like he's from a past era, which is why he kills every song he sings.

## 5. All I Want for Christmas is You by Fifth Harmony

By now, you should know all about Fifth Harmony. If you've never heard of them, this is a perfect song to hear first. They're a girl group with an amazing sound. You're going to love this version of All I Want For Christmas Is You as much (or maybe even more) than the original. These ladies really know how to sing.

## 6. Rockin' around the Christmas Tree by Miley Cyrus

You can say what you want about Miley, but she knows how to sing. A song like this is perfect for her, because it's fun and wild, just like she is. Even though most holiday songs are slow and soft, this one is the opposite. That's why you should put it on whenever you want to move around and party.

## 7. Winter Wonderland by Selena Gomez & the Scene

Selena Gomez is not only an amazing actress and a gorgeous woman, but she's also a talented singer. This song is fun and upbeat, which means it's bound to put you in a cheery mood. When you hear her voice, it'll be impossible to stop yourself from singing along.

Christmas music never gets boring. Whenever you're tired of listening to a classic song sung by the original singer, you can listen to a modern version of it. What's your favorite Christmas song?
