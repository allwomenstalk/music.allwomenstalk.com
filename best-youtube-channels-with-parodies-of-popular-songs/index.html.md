# 7 Best Youtube Channels with Parodies of Popular Songs ...

When you’re bored, why not watch some parodies of popular songs? You’ll recognize the tunes, but hear new words that will keep you laughing. It’s a great form of entertainment that you’ll want to watch over and over. Browse these YouTube channels filled with parodies of popular songs, and then show them off to your friends.

## 1. Bart Baker

Bart Baker performs parodies of popular songs like “Blurred Lines” by Robin Thicke and a bunch of different One Direction songs. His videos have great quality and take place on sets that look just like the original videos. He even has YouTube guest stars like Kingsley, Dave Days, and Shane Dawson. If you want to listen to your favorite catchy songs with altered lyrics, you have to check out his channel. You could spend hours watching all of the various videos he's created.

## 2. Barely Political

The Key of Awesome is a comedic musical group that sings songs on their Barely Political YouTube channel. They spoof songs like “We Can’t Stop” by Miley Cyrus and “Call Me Maybe” by Carly Rae Jepsen. Their most popular song is a parody of “Tik Tok” by Ke$ha and has over 100,000 views. They have lovely voices that make their videos as entertaining as possible. Check out their channel if you're interested in having a laugh.

## 3. Teddiefilms

This channel makes fun of music, television, and movies. My favorite of their parodies is their Breaking Bad version of Taylor Swift’s “We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together.” They also have videos that reference Star Wars and Les Miserables. If you’re a fan of the shows that they make fun of in their videos and don’t take things too seriously, you’re sure to love watching them. They even take requests, so if there's a show you love, let them know.

## 4. The Hillywood Show

Are you a huge movie buff that aims to watch every film that’s released? The individuals on this channel dress up as the actors in the film that they’re spoofing. They parody Twilight and The Hobbit by turning them into miniature musicals. If you love the movies, you’ll love this channel. So why not go listen?

## 5. Dave Days

Dave Days has an entire section of his channel dedicated to parodies of Miley Cyrus, Justin Timberlake, and other celebrities. One of his best videos is a parody of “I Just Had Sex,” by The Lonely Island called “I Just Held Hands.” He has a wonderful voice and works hard on every video he puts out. Not to mention, he's pretty darn cute. If you give him a chance, you’ll end up loving him as much as everyone else does.

## 6. CollegeHumor

I’m sure you’ve heard of the website, but are you subscribed to their YouTube channel? They have tons of different comedy videos, which include parodies of songs. “I Gotta Feeling” and “Thrift Shop” are some of the many songs that they reference. Watch them on their channel, and then visit their website for more fun. With the amount of material they have, they'll provide you with hours of laughs.

## 7. Venetian Princess

Ready to watch a girl who’s both talented and gorgeous? She sings songs by Britney Spears, Lady Gaga, and other female pop stars. She’s sure to entertain you with her crazy outfits and song lyrics. Go browse her channel and become one of her new fans. What are you waiting for?

These are just some of the dozens of great parody channels out there. You can also check out Thecomputernerd01, keeptheheat, and Shane Dawson TV. What’s your favorite parody of a song?
