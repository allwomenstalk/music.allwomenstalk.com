# 7 Cool Bands to Listen to when You Want to Relax ...

You never know when something is going to completely wrack your nerves, so it’d be best to be equipped with bands to listen to when you want to relax. Not only will these band help soothe your ruffled nerves, but they’ll also make you feel calmer and more serene overall. Listen to these wonderful bands before you go to bed, practicing a relaxing activity like yoga, or even studying, and I bet they’ll lift your spirits in no time. Here are a few of my favorite bands to listen to when you want to relax; check them out below!

## 1. The Xx

I am totally in love with bands to listen to when you want to relax because I’m stressed pretty much every single day as a working, full-time college student in NYC. But, The xx makes my day that much more bearable. Their songs have such a soothing quality and give my ears something to be happy about with their ambient, airy, flowing beats and lovely lyrics. Among my favorite songs are “Angels,” "VCR," and “Sunset." Check them out if you haven’t already.

## 2. Bon Iver

If you haven’t acquainted yourself with the masterpiece called “Skinny Love,” you have really been missing out! It’s one of Bon Iver’s most popular songs. But don't forget to take a chance and delve into the other songs as well. Each has a relaxing tone and really sets a nice mood for something calming like yoga, or a nice candlelit bubble bath!

## 3. Coldplay

Coldplay is not by any means new to most people if you’ve ever listened to music. But, they’re certainly worth mentioning because they have such great hits with a sound that’s melodic, mellow, and soothing in the most beautiful way. They can calm you down and make you feel nice and even. Listen to them on your way to bed for calming dreams, ladies!

## 4. Mumford & Sons

Mumford & Sons have a folky, bluegrass feel to their music, which is not typically my thing. But, I must say, they do have some pretty calming songs on their album that I consider some of my personal faves, like “Not with Haste” and “The Cave.” Although they do have their fair share of fast paced songs, even those tend to make your feel at ease too. They’ve really changed my outlook on that genre of music.

## 5. Cold War Kids

If you like your relaxing music to have more of a rock beat, then the Cold War Kids are certainly a good choice. Their lead singer’s vocals are simply wonderful and when paired with their bluesy, soulful sound, it creates a beautifully soothing, relaxing atmosphere. I’ve listened to them when I’ve been stressed out and they have definitely helped calmed me down in my frantic state. Among my favorite songs are “Audience” and “Tuxedos.”

## 6. Youth Lagoon

Youth Lagoon sometimes lets you linger in the moment in their songs with their minimalist, flighty sound. It's almost as if you’re wandering somewhere in a dream, absorbed in a calming world. I love that they pull you in with their music, that seems both magical and inspiring. My favorite song from them is “17,” but their other songs are just as magnificent.

## 7. Pink Floyd

This band is an oldie but a goodie. They are famous and in my opinion, classic in the music industry. Their music provides you with great tunes that are soothing, relaxing and they definitely put you at peace--there’s a reason that people are still talking about this band! Some of the songs on my "most played" list include "The Happiest Days of Our Lives” and “Wish You Were Here.” If you haven’t heard their music already, I urge you to check them out. It’ll be the one of the best sounds to calm your nerves you’ve encountered all day!

In this fast-paced world, it can be hard to get yourself in the mood to calm down, relax, and just get some quality me time. I guarantee if you listen to these bands while trying to sleep or do any other relaxing activity, you’ll enjoy it that much more! What are your favorite bands to listen to when you want to relax? Do you like any of these bands?
