# 9 High\-Energy Songs to Power Through Your Winter Workouts

Your workout playlist shouldn't be stagnant. Just as your routine evolves, your music needs to shift to keep your energy high. To help you stay motivated, here are some of the best tracks to power through your winter workouts.

## 1. WTF (Where They From)

[Missy Elliott](https://missy-elliott.com/) and Pharrell Williams teamed up for this high-energy anthem that is perfect for any cardio session.

## 2. Hide Away

Daya delivers a catchy, upbeat melody in this track that is sure to keep you moving on the treadmill.

## 3. Flesh without Blood

This track by [Grimes](https://www.grimesmusic.com/) offers a unique, driving rhythm to help you crush your fitness goals. If you're looking for more ways to stay active, check out our guide on [fitness tips for busy women](https://allwomenstalk.com/fitness-tips-for-busy-women/).

## 4. Adventure of a Lifetime

Coldplay brings a vibrant, infectious beat to this song that makes even the toughest intervals feel easier.

## 5. Good for You (Phantoms Remix)

Selena Gomez and A$AP Rocky created an inspiring track, and this remix by [Phantoms](https://www.phantoms-music.com/) adds an extra layer of intensity perfect for lifting weights.

## 6. Something Better

This track is a fantastic pick to keep your spirits up during a challenging workout. For more music inspiration, explore our list of [best upbeat songs for your morning routine](https://allwomenstalk.com/best-upbeat-songs-for-your-morning-routine/).
