# The 9 Best Songs to Power through Your Chilly Winter Workouts ...

You shouldn't listen to the same songs during your summer workouts during your winter workouts. That's why _Shape_ has created a list of the best songs to listen to during this time of year. Here they are:

## 1. WTF (Where They from)

This is by the amazing Missy Elliott and Pharrell Williams.

## 2. Hide Away

Daya sings this fun song.

## 3. Flesh without Blood

This one is by Grimes.

## 4. Adventure of a Lifetime

Here's a great song from Coldplay.

## 5. Good for You (Phantoms Remix)

This inspiring song is by Selena Gomez and A$AP Rocky.

## 6. Something Better

This song has both Audien and Lady Antebellum in it!

## 7. Lay It All on Me

This is by Rudimental, but it has the lovable Ed Sheeran in it, too!

## 8. I Can’t Feel My Face (Martin Garrix Remix)

This song, by The Weeknd, will keep your energy up.

## 9. Better when I’m Dancin’

This fun song is by Meghan Trainor.

What songs do you listen to during your workouts?
