# 7 Best Throwback Songs for when You Feel Nostalgic ...

Throwback songs are the best way to bring yourself back to a fun or nostalgic moment in your life. Sometimes, I love putting a throwback song on Pandora and jamming to throwback music! This puts me in the best mood. Here are some throwback songs that might remind you of something!

## 1. Wannabe by the Spice Girls

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/fV0QA3)
I think this song is one of the greatest out of throwback songs for every girl here! Wannabe is a great song to jam to in the car, at home, or with your girls. This song will be sure to bring you and your girls to a fun time in your lives! It definitely does for me. And you still hear this song until this day. Try the Spice Girls channel on Pandora and see how quickly it comes on!

## 2. It Wasn’t Me by Shaggy

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/it-wasnt-me-single/679030204)
This song is one of the best throwback jams. I love this song. On the rare occasion that this song plays on the radio, I just have to turn it up! The lyrics are too funny and it’s so catchy. I admit when I hear it in the car, I have to dance and drive. It’s one of my favorite throwbacks!

## 3. Say My Name by Destiny’s Child

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/say-my-name-ep/400079116)
Say My Name is one of the most popular Destiny’s Child songs. It still plays on the radio until this day. This song is my personal favorite by Destiny’s Child, and there are so many remixes. Survivor is also a great throwback. I’d jam to this one in the car, if you want to go full out Beyonce like I do!

## 4. I Want It That Way by Backstreet Boys

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/XsO831)
I loved boy bands growing up. N’Sync and Backstreet Boys were my favorites! I Want It That Way is a very popular song by the Backstreet Boys, which is why it's listed first on their Greatest Hits CD. I always jam to their Pandora. I also loved their performance at the VMA’s!

## 5. This is How We do It by Montell Jordan

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/this-is-how-we-do-it/1442959457)
This song can’t help but make me dance. This song is still used everywhere, especially at weddings! I love jamming to this. It’s one of my favorite throwbacks and songs to dance to. That will never change!

## 6. Tearin’ up My Heart by N’Sync

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/artist/nsync/398120)
This is such a beautiful song by N’Sync. It’s by far my favorite by them! I love singing this song in the car. If I knew how to sing WELL, the car wouldn’t be the only place I’d jam to it. Given the circumstances, I’ll stick to the car…

## 7. I Don’t Wanna Miss a Thing by Aerosmith

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/i-dont-want-to-miss-a-thing-ep/253426154)
Last, but not least, the famous song, I Don’t Wanna Miss a Thing. This is one of the songs that is still played all the time, and I love it when it plays during "Armageddon." Remember that scene? It’s such a powerful song and so very sweet! I love jamming to this song. It always makes me happy.

Those were my favorite throwback songs! I hope you guys enjoyed them and give them a listen. Throwback music is the best way to bring back some great memories! What are some of your favorite throwbacks?
