# This Highly\-anticipated Soundtrack is Shaping up to Be One of the Best Youll Hear ...

Lorde's one of the biggest names in the music biz right now and she's doing a pretty incredible job with her gig as the curator for The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Part 1's soundtrack. "Curating the soundtrack for such a hotly anticipated film was a challenge, but I jumped at the chance," she said in a press release. " I think the soundtrack is definitely going to surprise people." I have no doubt about that! This week, she released the full track list and it includes musical A-listers like Charli XCX, Haim, Grace Jones and some very note-worthy up and coming artists. Here's a look at the track list:

**Meltdown:** by Stromae feat. Lorde, Pusha T, Q-Tip, Haim
**Dead Air:** by Chvrches
**Scream My Name:** by Tove Lo
**Kingdom:** by Charli XCX feat. Simon Le Bon
**Track 5:** by Various Artists
**Lost Souls:** by Raury
**Yellow Flicker Beat:** by Lorde
**The Leap:** by Tinashe
**Plan the Escape:** by Bat for Lashes
**Original Beast:** by Grace Jones
**Flicker (Kanye West Rework):** by Lorde
**Animal:** by XOV
**This Is Not a Game:** by The Chemical Brothers feat. Miguel
**Ladder Song:** by Lorde

In case you missed it, Lorde already released one of the album's tracks. Her very own, "Yellow Flicker Beat." Take a listen right here!
