# The Fastest Way I Improved at Finding Great Music: The Hunger Games Soundtrack

Lorde is undoubtedly one of the biggest names in the music industry right now, and she is doing an incredible job in her role as the curator for _The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Part 1_ soundtrack. "Curating the soundtrack for such a hotly anticipated film was a challenge, but I jumped at the chance," she shared in a recent press release. "I think the soundtrack is definitely going to surprise people." There is no doubt about that! This week, the full track list was unveiled, featuring musical A-listers like Charli XCX, Haim, Grace Jones, and several noteworthy up-and-coming artists.

The soundtrack promises to be a diverse sonic journey, blending established icons with fresh voices. If you're looking for more cinematic vibes, check out our list of [the best movie soundtracks of all time](https://music.allwomenstalk.com/the-best-movie-soundtracks-of-all-time/).
