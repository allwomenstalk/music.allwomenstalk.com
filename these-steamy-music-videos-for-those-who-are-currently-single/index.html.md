# These 17 Steamy Music Videos for Those Who Are Currently Single ...

Music has the power to make you feel things. A song can make you happy, angry, heartbroken, or even kind of randy. Music videos aren't yet obsolete, either. They give you a visual representation of the songs you love, which can often make you appreciate them even more. So, guess what I have for you here? Some visual stimulation, if you will. These songs are hot, but the videos are hotter. Like, if you're single, you might want to watch these alone and spend some quality time by yourself.

This starts out sweet, so you might wonder why I qualify it as steamy. Because of nudity, of course.

## 2. Kiss It Better – Rihanna

Rihanna does sexy, steamy, and scandalous so very well. She sings such powerful songs, but she also knows how to get in touch with her sensuality – especially in her videos. Like this one. Damn, y'all.

## 3. Work – Rihanna Feat. Drake

I ship these two so hard. So very, very hard.

## 4. Hands to Myself – Selena Gomez

I think the title of the song explains why this video is so hot. I mean … wow, Selena, you go right on ahead.

## 5. Justify My Love – Madonna

Over the years, Madonna has put out a butt load of songs and accompanying videos that were charged enough to cause a sexual awakening in anyone listening or watching, but the video of “Justify My Love” is next level steamy.

## 6. Dirrty – Christina Aguilera

Christina definitely got dirrty. She owned it, she owned her sexuality, and she owned her sex appeal. Get it, Xtina.

## 7. I'm a Slave 4 U – Britney Spears

Oh, Brit. Ohhh, Brit. Remember when she used to wear pigtails and school uniforms? This song marked such a difference.

## 8. She Wolf – Shakira

Shakira makes every video steamy just by appearing in it.

## 9. Ex's and Oh's – Elle King

Punctuation aside, Elle King filled this video with buff, oily bodies. It's a great song, but the video itself is total eye candy.

## 10. Sexyback – Justin Timberlake

This is the song that made me fall in love with Justin Timberlake. I played this on repeat for an entire summer and watched the video so often I … well. Never mind.

## 11. Naughty Girl – Beyonce

Yes, Bey. Yes, you are – and I, for one, would not have it any other way.

## 12. Body on Me – Rita Ora Feat. Chris Brown

So, Chris is problematic and right now, Rita's having some issues with the Beyhive, but I mean. This video, man. This video.

## 13. Fire Meet Gasoline – Sia

Heidi Klum is responsible for making the video so sexy. The song itself is fire solely because of Sia, though.

## 14. Alejandro – Lady Gaga

Gaga's sexiness isn't always obvious, even in her videos. There is nothing subtle about the massive amounts of steam heat in the vid for “Alejandro,” though.

## 15. Crazy – Aerosmith

Liv Tyler and Alicia Silverstone. That's all I have to say.

I think this video is sexy. But then, I think empowerment is sexy. I think power is sexy. I think confidence is sexy. I could choose any number of Bey's songs (I mean, besides the one I already included), but I pick this one.
