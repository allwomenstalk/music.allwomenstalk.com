Badass Songs to Listen to when Youre Getting Ready ...

By Lyndsie

Getting ready for the day – or the evening – can be … well, it depends, really. Sometimes it's fun, sometimes it's fast, and sometimes it's just tiresome. I feel that way occasionally when I'm getting ready to go out. There are days when I just need a little inspiration as I apply a fierce face and do something fabulous with my hair. You know how I give myself a boost? Music! These are the songs I listen to when I'm getting pretty. Add yours!

1. Florence + the Machine – Kiss with a Fist

I … don't know why I like listening to this when I get ready, but I do. There's something a little punk about, plus I admit, I love the lyrics.

2. Beyoncé – Who Run the World?

GIRLS.

3. Prince Ft. Sheena Easton – U Got the Look

This is particularly fitting in light of Prince's recent, tragic passing. RIP, Prince. Thank you for making us all feel a little more fabulous.

4. Fergie Ft. Ludacris – Glamorous

Fergie makes me dance around the bathroom. I fully admit that I sing into my brush and pose in the mirror. Sometimes I even kiss my reflection.

5. Hozier – Take Me to Church

I've loved this song since I first saw the heartbreaking video, but the song itself also has the power to make me feel … well, like I'm the woman Hozier's singing about, actually.

6. Third Eye Blind – Semi-Charmed Life

This has been a go-to for me since high school. That beat, man. That gets me excited about getting ready.

7. TLC – No Scrubs

Same here. I don't look for boys when I go out, scrubs or otherwise (I'm married to a sugar momma, I don't need no scrubs), but this song still puts a strut in my step.

8. Lady Gaga – Marry the Night

To be honest, any of Gaga's songs will make you feel like a queen as you're getting ready to walk out the door. This one's just my favorite.

9. Shakira – She Wolf

Shakira just … god, I love her.

10. Christina Aguilera Feat. Lil’ Kim – Can’t Hold Us down

Regretfully, I am not as into Christina as I once was. That being said, this song does things to me. It puts a little extra pep in my step.

11. Rihanna – Hard

Rhi does, too. She can make a girl feel so frigging empowered.

12. Katy Perry – Part of Me

What? I can't explain it.

13. The Pussycat Dolls – I Don’t Need a Man

Ahahaha, I really don't. You don't either.

14. Queen – Don't Stop Me Now

Queen will never not make me feel awesome.

15. Panic! at the Disco – La Devotee

Everyone needs a song like this on their makeup playlist, even if it's not this particular song.

16. Taylor Swift – Shake It off

As you're applying your favorite cosmetics and feeling your look, remember this: if you don't have a single hater, you're doing something wrong.

17. Demi Lovato – Confident

This goes without saying, right?

18. Beyoncé – Single Ladies

Of course, that was before Lemonade dropped. I think Bey is going to feature even more heavily on my playlist.

How do you rock out when you're putting on makeup and getting ready to step out?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some killer songs to listen to while doing my makeup?
Oh, there are so many amazing tracks! Think about songs that make you feel powerful and confident. Stuff like Beyoncé's 'Flawless', Lizzo's 'Good as Hell', or Rihanna's 'Needed Me'. These are sure to give you that fierce energy!
Any recommendations for songs that give a major makeover vibe?
Totally! You want tracks that pump you up and make you feel ready for transformation. Try 'Man! I Feel Like a Woman!' by Shania Twain, 'I'm Every Woman' by Whitney Houston, or even 'New Rules' by Dua Lipa. They'll get you in that makeover mood for sure!
Are there any songs specifically about makeup and beauty?
You bet! One classic is 'Lip Gloss' by Lil Mama. Also, check out 'Make Up' by Ariana Grande. These songs celebrate beauty in a fun and sassy way, perfect for your beauty routine.
What should I listen to if I'm getting ready to go out for a night of fun?
You need something hype! Try 'Uptown Funk' by Mark Ronson featuring Bruno Mars, 'Levitating' by Dua Lipa, or 'Party in the U.S.A.' by Miley Cyrus. These songs are guaranteed to get you in a party mood!
Can you suggest some songs that are great for makeup tutorial videos?
Absolutely! You want something catchy but not too distracting. Consider songs like 'Royals' by Lorde, 'Fashion' by Lady Gaga, or 'Scar Tissue' by Red Hot Chili Peppers. They create the perfect background vibe for your tutorials!
