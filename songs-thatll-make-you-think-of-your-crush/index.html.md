# Songs Thatll Make You Think of Your Crush ...

It's fun to fantasize about your crush and all of the fun things you'd do as a couple. Even though he hasn't realized that the two of you are perfect for each other yet, he'll figure it out eventually. Until he does, you should feel free to spend your time daydreaming about the moment when he'll finally ask you out. While you're fantasizing over the future, here are a few cute songs about crushes to listen to:

## 1. Crush by Mandy Moore

This is a sweet song that'll make you melt. You can sing along to it as you're looking through your crush's Facebook page or daydreaming about what your first kiss will be like. The lyrics describe crushes pretty perfectly. After all, don't you get a rush when you're around that one boy and wonder if he feels the same? Mandy Moore knows how all of us girls think and this song proves it.

## 2. Pumpkin Soup by Kate Nash

Here's a song for you to listen to when you're in the mood for a little loving. Of course, it's even better to sing along to if you've fallen for someone who is hot, but doesn't really have the personality to match, because the lyrics talk about wanting a kiss, not an actual relationship.

## 3. Crush by David Archuleta

Falling for a close friend is always tricky. You don't want to admit that you have feelings for them and lose them as a friend. But you don't want to miss out on your chance with them either. That's what this song is all about, so listen to the lyrics closely if you're in a similar situation. David Archuleta might just inspire you to make a move.

## 4. Falling for You by Colbie Caillat

Here's a song to listen to when you're just starting to fall for a boy. Even though you know you should wait until you get to know him better to make a move, it's hard to hold yourself back. After all, you can already tell that he's the one for you.

## 5. What I like about You by the Romantics

This is a classic song that you might've heard on the radio. It lists all of the reasons why a guy has fallen for a girl. He likes everything about her, and you probably feel the same way when you look at your crush, don't you?

## 6. He Could Be the One by Hannah Montana

You have to admit that Miley Cyrus sang some catchy songs as Hannah Montana. Whether you loved or hated the show, you should be able to appreciate this song about a special boy who might just be the love of Hannah's life.

## 7. Happy with Me by Holy Child

Don't you just want your crush to be happy with you? Life is filled with a million annoying chores that you have to get done, so you deserve to have a little fun with a special someone. After all, you can't be expected to work all the time without having any time to play. This song will inspire you to walk right up to your crush and do what you have to do.

Whether or not you end up with your crush, you're free to daydream about him as often as you'd like. That's the beauty of the brain. What other songs make you think of your crush?
