# 7 Acoustic Performances by Little Mix Thatll Leave You in Awe ...

There are so many incredible acoustic performances by Little Mix. I recently saw them perform live opening up for Demi Lovato on her Neon Lights tour and I was so impressed! I definitely consider myself a "Mixer" (what their fans call themselves) now. I've spent a lot of time watching their videos and these acoustic performances by Little Mix are some of my favorites! Check them out and then let me know which ones are your favorites!

## 1. Holy Grail Mash-up

This video gives me chills! The girls performed a mash-up of Justin Timberlake & Jay Z's "Holy Grail," One Republic's "Counting Stars" and Nirvana's "Smells Like Teen Spirit." They performed it live at BBC's Live Lounge and it's amazing. Their voices harmonize so beautifully together and their solo parts are just as impressive. The video has racked up nearly 2 million views! This is just one of the awesome acoustic performances by Little Mix.

## 2. About the Boy

This video has racked up close to half a million views! The girls performed it live during their album listening party for "Salute." As always, the girls do not disappoint. I love how effortless it looks for them - how can they sound THAT good?! It's definitely an awesome thing to see them perform and see them get so into the performance. I love that about artists!

## 3. We Are Young

The girls performed an acoustic cover of Fun's "We Are Young" and they sound amazing! But then again, what else is new? They toned down the vibe of the song to make it more of an R&B ballad - I might even like it better than the original! I love how the girls sound fantastic individually, but together, they're incomparable!

## 4. How Ya Doin'

I love this cover! They performed it live on Sunday Brunch and can I just say WOW at Jesy's beatboxing (at 2:10)?! The girls even performed this acoustically on the Neon Lights tour! It's definitely become one of my favorite performances.

## 5. Move

I love the studio version of this song but I love the acoustic version even more! It's amazing how great they sound live! The girls performed their single, "Move," live in Nova's Red Room and sound great. I love the different ad-libs they add to their songs when they perform it live. It's very impressive!

## 6. Doo-Wop/ Never Leave You Mash-up

This is another mash-up that I can't get enough of! The girls performed "Doo-Wop" and "Never Leave You" for SBTV and I love how they mixed both songs! The video has racked up nearly one million views - no surprise there!

## 7. Wings

I can't get enough of this song. It's such a feel-good song that just screams girl power! They performed it at the Neon Lights tour and the crowd went wild! I love that it's a big pop song, but I love that they tone it down for an acoustic performance. It's a more intimate feel and still manages to deliver the same message.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many of Little Mix's incredible acoustic performances. Every time I watch these, I'm blown away by how talented they are! Have you seen them perform live yet? Of the list, which is your favorite acoustic performance?
