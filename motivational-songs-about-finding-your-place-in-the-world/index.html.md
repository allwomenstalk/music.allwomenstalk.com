# 7 Motivational Songs about Finding Your Place in the World ...

When you're struggling with life, music is the cure you're looking for. After listening to a few appropriate songs, you'll have an entirely different mindset. Here are the most motivational songs about finding your place in the world:

## 1. Me against the World by Simple Plan

If you feel like the world is against you, this is the song for you. It'll remind you that you don't have to give in to what society tries to peer pressure you to do. You should be your own person and make your own decisions, and forget all about what others want you to do. It's your life, after all. Live it in a way that makes you happy.

## 2. Place in This World by Taylor Swift

This is an older Taylor Swift song, but it's just as amazing as her newer ones. It's all about her being alone, but having enough strength to continue living out her life. She doesn't know what her place in the world is, but she won't stop until she figures it out. Sure, she'll make mistakes, but that's all part of the journey.

## 3. Where I Belong by Hometown

If you love boy bands, you'll go crazy over these kids. They'll remind you that sometimes the place where you belong isn't in a certain town or city--it's in someone's arms. Once you meet the love of your life, you'll feel like you've finally found the place where you belong. It can be that simple.

## 4. Who I Am by Jessica Andrews

If you have big dreams, you need to pay attention to the message in this song. It reminds you that you should be happy, even if you don't end up achieving your goals. While you should try your hardest to be successful, you should also find happiness in what you already have.

## 5. The Middle by Jimmy Eat World

This song is for all the women out there that are trying to figure out where they belong. There's no need to freak out about finding a place in this world, because "it just takes some time." You can't expect to find your life's calling as soon as you start looking for it. You just need to "live right now and be yourself," and eventually everything will fall into place.

## 6. This is Me by Demi Lovato

It's hard not to love Demi. She gets better by the second, but that doesn't mean that her old stuff isn't worth listening to. Every girl should be able to relate to this upbeat song. It's perfect to listen to when you're in a great mood. It'll remind you that you're exactly where you're supposed to be at this moment in time. Plus, it features Joe Jonas, and who doesn't want to hear his lovely voice?

## 7. Somewhere I Belong by Linkin Park

This is a great song to listen to if you've been struggling in life and are ready to heal. It'll give you the motivation to keep going, even though times are rough. The lyrics even say, "I want to let go of the pain I've held so long." So listen to Linkin Park and let your worries fly away.

Never underestimate the power of music. It can turn your world around. What other motivational songs do you have on repeat?
