# 7 Songs Thatll Remind You Itll All Be Okay ...

The rest of your days won't be as crummy as the past few days were. Every once in a while, life will get messy for you, but it'll end up working itself out. You need to have faith that things will get better with time. Since that can be a hard thing to believe, here are a few songs to remind you that it'll all be okay the next time you're feeling low:

## 1. It Gets Better Song by Todrick Hall

The lyrics to this song start about 56 seconds into the video. It's about kids who get bullied, and how you should keep your head up, even when others treat you poorly. You have to ignore the haters and never give up on yourself, because it truly does get better. Just wait and see for yourself.

## 2. Okay by Backhouse Mike

This song is sung in a relaxing way that will truly make you believe that everything will be okay. Even if the lyrics don't calm you down, the soothing music will. It's a great song to put on when you want to lay down and fall asleep without worrying about what will happen tomorrow.

## 3. Change by Taylor Swift

You might've heard this song before, because it's sung by the talented Taylor Swift. It's about a girl who knows she's capable of changing her life. She's sick of dealing with the same issues over and over, but she knows it'll all be okay at the end of the day, because things will change for her eventually. Listen to Taylor's advice, because what she's saying is completely true.

## 4. Getting Better by the Beatles

Take a look at your life and pay attention to the little things that have gotten better over the years. It can't all be bad, so there must be something that has been getting better. The Beatles will remind you to focus on those things instead of the bad things. It's all about the way you look at life.

## 5. It Gets Better by Fun

There have been some pretty inspirational songs by Fun, and this is one of them. The entire chorus consists of telling listeners that it will get better. It's something that you have to believe as you walk through life, because it'll help you survive even the toughest days.

## 6. It’s Getting Better by Cass Elliot

This song may be about a relationship, but you don't have to think about that fact. If you just focus on Cass Elliot singing about it getting better in her cheery tone, then it'll put you in a better mood, no matter what problems are plaguing your life.

## 7. I Want to Live by Josh Gracin

This song will encourage you to change the way you look at the world. Everything will be okay if you start actually living life instead of just stumbling through it. You need to make the conscious decision to do whatever it takes to create a life that you can be proud of. Don't let life beat you up. Take control of it.

Music is one of the best ways to alter your mood, so bring your iPod with you when you're worried about having a horrible day. What songs do you listen to when you want to be reassured that everything will be okay?
