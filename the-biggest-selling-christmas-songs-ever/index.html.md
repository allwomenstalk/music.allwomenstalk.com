# the Biggest Selling Christmas Songs Ever ...

Since there are now so many different ways to buy music it’s become difficult for statisticians to provide verifiable sales figures. It could be quite some time until we receive definitive rankings for highest selling music across all media but there’s no denying we can still identify some of the biggest selling and most played Christmas songs. Get your singing boots on ladies cos we‘re going to rock around the Christmas tree.

## 1. White Christmas by Bing Crosby

Arguably the most famous and most loved Christmas song of all time, you can’t think about this time of year without subconsciously humming this classic tune.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/white-christmas/398412331?app=itunes&i=398412536)

## 2. All I Want for Christmas is You by Mariah Carey

Mariah Carey could probably retire and never work again thanks to all of the royalties she gets from this classic Christmas song being played endlessly every single year!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/all-i-want-for-christmas-is-you/585972750?at=11l4BD&i=585972803&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 3. Rockin’ around the Christmas Tree by Brenda Lee

You can’t help but get up and dance when you hear this one; the smooth voice of Brenda Lee and an effortlessly cool old rock and roll vibe!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/rockin-around-christmas-tree/566038131?app=itunes&i=566038141)

## 4. Feliz Navidad by Jose Feliciano

This joyous Christmas song is not only entertaining but it’s also educational, letting your dance and sing and learn a little Spanish all at the same time!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrLqB)

## 5. Jingle Bell Rock by Bobby Helms

This one has always been an iconic Christmas song since its release in the 50s, and was given a fresh lease of life when it was covered in Mean Girls!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/jingle-bell-rock/264323152?app=itunes&i=264323200)

## 6. The Christmas Song by Nat King Cole

Can anybody in the world thing about Christmas without singing, “chestnuts roasting on an open fire” in their heads? Nat King Cole definitely made his mark on the festive period!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/christmas-song-feat-natalie/737347219?app=itunes&i=737347416)

## 7. Santa Baby by Eartha Kitt

Eartha Kitt makes Christmas sexy with this sultry smooth song. Who would have thought that a love letter to Santa could be so seductive!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNyz)

## 8. Last Christmas by Wham!

Christmas and 80s cheese - is that not just a combination made in heaven? Well, if you didn’t think so before, your mind will have almost certainly been changed the first time you heard this classic.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/last-christmas-single-version/202117886?at=11l4BD&i=202117984&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 9. Blue Christmas by Elvis Presley

Elvis Presley’s honey-like voice made any song an instant classic, and it was definitely the case for this Christmas song. Melancholy but fantastic.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrLGT)

## 10. Happy Xmas (War is over) by John Lennon

Not content with mastering the world of rock and roll, John Lennon also managed to release one of the best songs of all time too. What a clever guy!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/happy-xmas-war-is-over/913286780?app=itunes&i=913286801)

## 11. It’s the Most Wonderful of the Year by Andy Williams

There is something really comforting about this Andy Williams song. Perhaps it’s the nostalgia or just that is evokes the very happiest Christmas memories you can think of!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNyG)

## 12. Do They Know It’s Christmas by Band Aid

This great track killed two birds with one stone; it gave listeners an unforgettable Christmas song and it helped millions with the money that it raised.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/do-they-know-its-christmas/268804568?app=itunes&i=268804635)

## 13. Step into Christmas by Elton John

Elton John displays his signature piano prowess in this catchy Christmas tune; one of the better Christmas songs from the 1970s.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/step-into-christmas/264323152?app=itunes&i=264323154)

## 14. Wonderful Christmastime by Paul McCartney

John Lennon wasn’t the only Beatle who got in on the Christmas fun, with Paul McCartney giving us a festive favorite in the form of this catchy song.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNyL)

## 15. Christmas Wrapping by the Waitresses

This track by The Waitresses is probably one of the coolest Christmas songs out there. It’s not particularly cheesy; it’s just a great time!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/christmas-wrapping/15038588?app=itunes&i=15038573)

## 16. 2000 Miles by the Pretenders

Rock bands like The Pretenders don’t usually get involved with the Christmas music scene, but we’re certainly glad they decided to give it a go as 2000 Miles is an absolute gem.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNP0)

## 17. Christmas in Hollis by Run-DMC

If typical Christmas music isn’t your favorite, then try something a little different with some festive hip-hop from pioneering group Run-DMC!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNP2)

## 18. Where Are You Christmas? by Faith Hill

This is wonderful and touching song, sung by Faith Hill and used for the movie How The Grinch Stole Christmas.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrLH0)

## 19. 8 Days of Christmas by Destiny’s Child

Destiny’s Child added some much needed R&B flavor to the Christmas music scene when they released a whole themed album in the 2000s. This one is the pick of the bunch.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNP4)

## 20. Let It Snow, Let It Snow, Let It Snow by Dean Martin

This song has been covered dozens of times, but the best version will always be the original by the late great Dean Martin.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/let-it-snow-let-it-snow-let/268804568?app=itunes&i=268804737)

## 21. Santa Claus is Comin’ to Town by the Jackson 5

If you look at the lyrics, this song can actually be quite creepy, but you can’t do anything but dance and sing along when you hear the Jackson 5 version!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/santa-claus-is-coming-to-town/264323152?app=itunes&i=264323206)

## 22. Christmas (Baby Please Come Home) by Darlene Love

Best known for performing it every single year at Christmas on David Letterman’s show, Darlene Love’s classic is one of the most beloved festive tunes ever.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/christmas-baby-please-come-home/336036941?at=11l4BD&i=336037344&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 23. Little Saint Nick by the Beach Boys

The Beach Boys lend their signature, unmistakable surfer sound to this unconventional Christmas classic, so catchy!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/little-saint-nick/268804568?app=itunes&i=268804928)

## 24. Sleigh Ride by the Ronettes

The Ronettes have come to define the quintessential Phil Spector sound of the 60s, and it is put to fantastic use in this version of Sleigh Ride.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/sleigh-ride/336036941?at=11l4BD&i=336037282&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 25. Someday at Christmas by Stevie Wonder

Someday At Christmas is a pretty song that really makes you think about the world and how we could make it a better place.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrNPc)

## 26. The Magic of Christmas Day by Celine Dion

What could be better than one of the greatest singers ever providing you with the musical accompaniment to the best time of year? Not much!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/magic-christmas-day-god-bless/361308139?app=itunes&i=361308302)

## 27. Peace on Earth/the Little Drummer Boy by David Bowie and Bing Crosby

This one is so special; a meeting of different musical stars from different generations and genres, coming together to create a wonderful Christmas song.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/little-drummer-boy-peace-on/268804568?app=itunes&i=268804957)

## 28. Fairytale of New York by the Pogues and Kirsty MacColl

One of my absolute favorites. You can’t help but sing along to this Irish masterpiece, especially after a few drinks!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/fairytale-of-new-york-feat-kirsty-maccoll/107640150?at=11l4BD&i=107639519&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 29. Kelly Clarkson – underneath the Tree

Kelly’s 2013 Christmas album Wrapped In Red became an instant holiday favorite when it was released, equalling some of our long time classics.

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/underneath-the-tree/720861443?at=11l4BD&i=720861445&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 30. Leona Lewis – One More Sleep

X Factor winner Leona has been one of the few Brit stars to have made it big across the pond, and her Christmas song One More Sleep is a real winner!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://bit.ly/1ROrLXs)

## 31. Baby, It’s Cold outside by Michael Buble and Idina Menzel

You can’t complete a Christmas songs list with Buble! Have a listen to his duet with the amazing Idina Menzel, it’s a joy to hear!

Buy at: [geo.itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/baby-its-cold-outside-duet-with-michael-bubl%C3%A9/924313589?at=11l4BD&i=924313597&uo=4&app=itunes)

## 32. Merry ‘Xmas Everyone

If you’re British, like me, you simply can’t go through the holidays without hearing Noddy Holder belt out “It’s Christma-a-a-a-s “ at the end of this hugely catchy tune. Actually it was the most streamed Christmas song in 2104 and it is believed more than 40% of the world has heard this song.

Buy at: [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/merry-xmas-everybody-single/269642891)

## 33. I Believe in Father Christmas – Greg Lake

I’ve saved my absolute favorite ‘til last. You might imagine a jaunty little tune given the title, but it’s actually a hauntingly beautifully melodic tune.
