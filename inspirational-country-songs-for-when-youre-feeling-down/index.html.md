# 7 Inspirational Country Songs for when Youre Feeling down ...

When life gets you down, listening to some inspirational country songs can really help. Many times a great song can make you smile, make laugh and help you look at your current situation in a different light. If you’re a bit down right now, here’s 7 great inspirational country songs for you to listen to. I hope these songs help brighten your day.

## 1. “It’s a Great Day to Be Alive” by Travis Tritt

"It's A Great Day To Be Alive" is one of the best inspirational country songs to listen to when you're feeling down. We all have bad days and life doesn't always go the way we plan. This song focuses on looking at everything good about your life and being thankful for it. It shows us to enjoy today instead of worrying about tomorrow.

## 2. “ Bless the Broken Road” by Rascal Flatts

Several different singers have released their own versions of “ Bless The Broken Road”. However, it didn't become a hit until it was released by Rascal Flatts. This song shows us that we all go through bad things in our lives, but many of the struggles that we go through are worth it. If you need some extra inspiration Rascal Flatts has several inspiring songs.

## 3. "Live like You Were Dying" by Tim McGraw

What would you do if you were told that you only had a short time to live? This song tells the story of a man who is told exactly that. Instead of letting this devastating news stop him, he decides to live life to it’s fullest. He makes changes in his life to become a better husband, a better friend and to finally forgive others.

## 4. "Life Ain't Always Beautiful" by Gary Allan

Gary Allan is someone who experienced unimaginable pain in his own life when his beloved wife committed suicide a few years ago. “Life Ain't Always Beautiful” shows us that life can be very difficult at times. However, it's important to remember that it can be wonderful, as well and you should enjoy every moment that you can.

## 5. "Don’t Blink" by Kenny Chesney

I love songs that tell stories and this one is awesome. “Don't Blink” tells the story of a man who is interviewed on his birthday. This man, who 102 years old is asked about the secret to life. Instead of telling the reporter things he wished that he would have changed, the man simply says “don't blink”. This song shows us to live each day to its fullest.

## 6. "This One’s for the Girls" by Martina McBride

This song is proof that that inspirational songs can be fun. “This One's For The Girls” is a song that celebrates women of all ages. It shows that whether you're in high school, starting a new career or have become a middle-aged woman, you are beautiful and should be proud of who you are.

## 7. “I Hope You Dance” by Lee Ann Womack

“I Hope You Dance” is a beautiful song. There are times in our lives when we avoid doing things because we are afraid of what will happen. This song reminds us not to sit around and wait because we are afraid. If we don't life will pass us by.

I love inspirational songs. They can quickly change your perspective and make you think of your life differently. Do you have a favorite inspirational song? If so, I would love to know what it is.
