# 7 Reasons to Be Excited for Fall out Boy's Upcoming Tour ...

There are countless reasons why you should be excited about Fall Out Boy's upcoming tour. I for one, am ecstatic about it. I've been waiting for so long to finally see the band live! I nearly cried tears of joy when I got tickets to their PA show this summer. It sold out super quick, so if you managed to snag some tickets, consider yourself lucky! Check out the reasons why you should be excited for Fall Out Boy's upcoming tour.

## 1. The Opening Acts

Fall Out Boy's upcoming tour is featuring Paramore and New Politics as the opening acts. How incredible is that? Every performance is going to be amazing. The night's going to be full of great music and I can't wait to hear it. If for some unexplainable reason if you haven't heard the music from these opening acts, you need to do that. Like, now.

## 2. Summer

For some reason, concerts just seem to be a whole lot better when they're in the summer. There's something about spending a summer night with friends listening to your favorite music. That's why I'm super excited that FOB's tour will be happening in the summer. What a perfect time for some rock n' roll music. Am I right?!

## 3. Their Music

I loved every single track off of their Save Rock & Roll album. Seriously. I can't wait to hear them sing it live! I've spent so much time watching YouTube concert footage wishing that I was in the audience and now I get to finally be there! Oh, and I'm also keeping my fingers crossed that they sing some old school stuff too.

## 4. Performance

If you've spent as much time as I have looking up footage of their concerts, you know that Fall Out Boy puts on a killer performance. From what I've seen from their last tour, now is no different. Pete Wentz has already tweeted about expecting the unexpected on this upcoming tour. I hope they go all out - with everything. I'll be in the front row dying from excitement. No really, I might just die.

## 5. Eye Candy

Let's be real, Fall Out Boy is full of cute members. I mean, Patrick Stump?! Swoon. I can watch him sing all day. I'll definitely be taking pictures of him during the show. Yup, I'm gonna make it my phone wallpaper and everything. Let's not even get started on Pete Wentz.

## 6. The Pictures

This upcoming tour is going to be full of great photo ops. From the stage, opening acts, and the crowd - you'll get some killer photos. Concert pictures are my favorite. They always capture people at the perfect moment, when they're lost in the music. As much as I love watching the performer, I love watching the crowd. I can't even imagine what they feel every night on stage, looking out on everybody.

## 7. Memories

At the end of the night, you'll be thanking Fall Out Boy for the memories. Ha, see what I did there? But really, if you go to the show with a group of friends, you'll all have a stash of stories to tell. You can even go to the show alone. You don't need company to listen to great music and make awesome memories!

Well, there you have it! These are seven out of many reasons to be excited for Fall Out Boy's upcoming tour. I am too excited for words. I'll be seeing them in PA! Did you get tickets already? If so, what are you most looking forward to?
