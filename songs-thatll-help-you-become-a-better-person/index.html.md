# 7 Songs Thatll Help You Become a Better Person ...

Even though it's important for you to love yourself, it's also important for you to constantly try to better yourself. After all, we never stop growing and learning, which is why we naturally become more experienced people as we continue to age. Here are a few inspirational songs that'll make you want to become an even better person than you are now:

## 1. Man in the Mirror by Michael Jackson

There's nothing wrong with admitting that you need to make a change in your life. In fact, it's a great thing to say to yourself! It means that you're determined to become the best person you can be. This song, sung by the great Michael Jackson, will remind you that the best way to change the world is by starting with the person staring back at you in the mirror. Before you can help others, you need to help yourself.

## 2. I Wanna Get Better by Bleachers

This music video has a super long introduction, so skip to 1:10 if you'd just like to hear the actual song. Once it starts playing, you'll probably recognize it. Even if you don't, you should appreciate it, because it'll urge you to become the best person you can be. We all have our flaws, but we can always work on them.

## 3. If Today Was Your Last Day by Nickleback

We all make fun of Nickleback, but they've had some pretty inspirational songs. This is one of them, because it'll remind you that you're in control of your own life. If you knew you were going to die tomorrow, what would you do today? Would you call up your crush to admit your feelings? Would you apologize to your parents for what you've done to them in the past? If you'd do it on your last day, maybe you should do it today.

## 4. Tragic Magic by Falling in Reverse

This song is about a former drug addict who almost killed himself a few years back. However, he was able to turn tragedy into melody, which has helped his career take off. Try to follow his lead by channeling all of your pain into creative energy. It might just help you become successful.

## 5. The Climb by Miley Cyrus

Remember when Miley sang this song? It's about a woman who is trying to become successful, but is worried it will never happen. However, the ending point isn't what you should focus on. Sometimes, "the climb" is the best part of life, so don't stress yourself out to much by thinking about the future. Just focus on becoming a better you.

## 6. You Gotta Be by Des’ree

This song will push you to become stronger. It'll remind you that you need to be tough in order to get through life. Sometimes, it'll be hard, but you'll be able to do it if you believe.

## 7. Annie by Safetysuit

Even though it's great that you're trying to better yourself, this song will remind you that you're amazing just the way you are. Even though you may struggle to see your beauty, others can see it clearly. We're all just like Annie, because we don't realize how wonderful we are.

It's possible to love yourself, even though you're trying to better yourself. What other songs have helped you become a better person?
