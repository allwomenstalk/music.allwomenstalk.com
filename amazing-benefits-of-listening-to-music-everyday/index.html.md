# 7 Amazing Benefits of Listening to Music Everyday ...

If you're a music lover like me, you won't be surprised to know that there are some amazing benefits of listening to music. I find myself jamming to music while I complete many of my daily tasks. Some of my favorite times to listen to music are when I clean, cook, exercise, and drive. If you'd like to see how music impacts your life, here's a list of benefits of listening to music for you to check out. See if there's anything that you would like to add to the list.

## 1. Stress Reliever

One of the most amazing benefits of listening to music is that it's a great way to relieve stress. As busy women our lives can be very stressful. Listening to music allows you to take a little time to unwind and relax. For me, listening to music provides me with a little bit of time for myself. Sometimes just listening to one song when I'm stressed out can make a huge difference.

## 2. Improves Your Mood

If you're in a bad mood, listening to music is a great way to pep you up. There have been times when I've been down because of a situation in my life and found that music really helped me. Music allows you to hear stories about someone else's life. It shows you that you are not alone and that there's someone out there who's in / has been in a similar situation as you. It also allows you to see that there are people who may be dealing with worse situations than you are.

## 3. Keeps Your Heart Healthy

Did you know that listening to music is good for your heart? In fact listening to music for just 30-45 minutes every day is an easy way to strengthen your heart. When you listen to music, it's helps increase the blood flow to your heart, helping it become stronger. Some studies show that music can have the same effect on the body as many aerobic exercises. When you combine music and exercise together, you'll have a winning combination. Always remember to speak to your doctor before starting any exercise program.

## 4. Improves Your Memory

Listening to music is a great way to improve your memory. Many schools are now incorporating music into their curriculum because of this. Some schools even play music while students are taking their exams. Many students find that listening to music while they study, helps them learn the study material much quicker. If you listen to music while you study, be sure to avoid songs that could be a distraction.

## 5. Helps You Sleep

If you're having a difficult time sleeping, try listening to music. It's important to find tracks that are slow and soothing. Songs that are loud, may actually wake you up. When I go to bed, I usually have a ton of things floating around in my head that make It difficult for me to fall asleep quickly. I worry about oversleeping and all of the things I need to do the next day. Music helps clear my mind, so I can relax and fall asleep faster.

## 6. Increases Productivity

This is one of the main reasons I like to listen to music. When I'm cleaning, I feel that music motivates me to clean faster. Listening to music while you workout can also help motive you to push yourself a little harder. For me, listening to some tunes while I exercise provides me with a distraction. This is especially helpful if I'm doing a workout routine that I don't really like or on days that I don't want to exercise at all.

## 7. Helps Create Lasting Memories

Most people have a few songs that bring back great memories. It can be a tune that was played when they were a child or one they enjoyed as an adult. Perhaps, you have a tune that reminds you of an experience you shared with a friend or one that makes you think of your significant other. You may have some songs that trigger memories of a great trip that you took. There can also be songs that bring back bad memories from the past, but it may be best to switch those off when they come on.

I am thrilled to know that there are so many benefits of listening to music. Do you like listening to music? If so, when is your favorite time to listen to music. I can't wait to hear from you.
