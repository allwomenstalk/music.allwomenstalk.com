# This Lord of the Rings Star Just Released a Heavy Metal Christmas Single! ...

Christopher Lee might be 92 years old but he's still got some rock to let out! The Lord of the Rings/Star Wars actor just released a new Christmas single, "Darkest Carols, Faithful Sing." "At my age, the most important thing for me is to keep active by doing things that I truly enjoy," he said. "I do not know how long I am going to be around, so every day is a celebration and I want to share it with my fans." Naturally, as soon as I heard this news, I just HAD to listen to the song:

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/darkest-carols-faithful-sing/947838613)

"It's light-hearted, joyful and fun," Lee shared. "It's a band singing on how metal should be played, the effect it has on the band and its listeners." What do you think of the song? Will you be adding it to your holiday playlist?
