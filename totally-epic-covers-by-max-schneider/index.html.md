# 7 Totally Epic Covers by Max Schneider ...

If you're ever on YouTube with nothing to do, look up covers by Max Schneider. Trust me, you'll be more than impressed! He takes modern songs and makes them his own style. His YouTube channel actually includes a lot of collabs with other singers, and mash-ups of his fave songs! It was hard to keep this list restricted to 7 but I do think these 7 covers by Max Schneider will leave you in awe. If you like what you hear, check out his channel for more!

## 1. Hold on We're Going Home (Drake Cover)

This is one of the most recent covers by Max Schneider. The thing that really caught my attention is his incredible falsetto. He takes this song to levels where you didn't know it could go. Not to mention Kurt Schneider's (who, believe it or not, is not related to Max) amazing piano skills. I love when they pair up to make a new cover! They make a really great team, don't you think?

## 2. Hey There Delilah (Plain White T's Cover)

First, I'd like to acknowledge Max's beautiful, beautiful biceps. I know I'm not the only one who noticed them! But, back to the music. There's nothing I love more than an acoustic rendition of a song. I love that all Max does is pick up his ukulele and jam out. He's such a good performer!

## 3. Counting Stars (One Republic Cover)

This song is definitely a hard one to sing. Ryan Tedder (lead singer of One Republic) has an amazing vocal range! However, I think Max pulls it off fantastically. In this video he collabs with Danny Padilla, whose beat boxing is truly impressive. Again, Max shows off his falsetto voice, which just goes to show that he can sing just about anything.

## 4. Beneath Your Beautiful (Labrinth/Emeli Sande Cover)

This really is such a beautiful song. Max's voice paired with Liz Gillie is incredible. They've paired up before, when they covered "Somewhere Only We Know." You can find that on his channel! Max starts off the song with his calm, soothing voice. I love that he gets into every song that he sings and you can always see the emotional connection that he has with it.

## 5. Avicii/Chris Brown Mashup

Max took Avicii's "Wake Me Up" and Chris Brown's "Don't Wake Me Up" and made an awesome acoustic smashup. I personally love acoustic performances because I think that's where the talent is shown. Clearly, Max has it. Does anyone else love the fact that he plays the ukulele? I sure do.

## 6. Radioactive (Imagine Dragons Cover)

I love love love this. Max performed this in Ryan Seacrest's studio! I think this rock song was a bit out of Max's element but he still did a fantastic job with it. Which is what amazing singers do, right? They pull off any genre.

## 7. Waiting on the World to Change (John Mayer Cover)

Anyone that covers John Mayer is alright with me. Max did an awesome job with this song. I'm so glad I got to hear him sing it live during his summer tour last year. He truly loves his fans and always tries to interact with them during his performances.

There you have it! These awesome covers by Max Schneider will turn you into a major fan if you weren't already. These 7 aren't the only great ones (literally everything he posts is amazing). If you liked these, check out his channel! Which cover was your favorite?
