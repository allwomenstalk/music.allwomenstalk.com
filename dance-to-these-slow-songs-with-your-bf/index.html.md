# 9 Sweet Slow Dance Songs to Dance to with Your BF ...

Don't you love watching couples in romantic comedies slow dance in their living rooms? Well, you don't have to be jealous of a fictional character's love life, because you can do the same thing yourself. All you need is the perfect song, because it's hard to slow dance to most of the music that pops up on the radio. If you're in need of fitting songs, here are a few slow ones that you can dance to with your boyfriend:

## 1. Nothing like You and I by the Perishers

Here's a sweet song that's lovely to listen to while slow dancing with your boyfriend. You're going to feel like the lyrics explain your relationship, because there's nothing like the two of you. After all, every couple is unique.

## 2. First Day of My Life by Bright Eyes

Not all slow songs are boring and bland. This one is pretty upbeat, but it's still great to slow dance to. After all, it has an acoustic feel to it, which is utterly romantic. Plus, if you listen to the lyrics, then you're going to melt. It's the epitome of adorable.

## 3. Can't Help Falling in Love by Elvis Presley

There's nothing wrong with listening to the classics. Elvis was the biggest name back in his day, and for good reason. After all, he created soft songs like this that are perfect to listen to with your partner. It'll make you feel like you're falling in love with him all over again.

## 4. Everything Has Changed by Taylor Swift

There are some songs that have been hits on pop radio that still work well when it comes to slow dancing. You've probably heard this tune a dozen times before, but it never stops being romantic.

## 5. Say Something by a Great Big World

Now, this song may not be the happiest one in this great big world, but it certainly sounds sweet. If you ignore the lyrics, then swaying to the beat of it with your boyfriend will feel about as romantic as it gets.

## 6. Unconditionally by Katy Perry

Most of Katy's music is catchy, and this song is no different. However, unlike her other songs, this one is a bit slower, making it perfect for slow dancing. If you're in a serious relationship, then the lyrics will even describe the way you feel about your boyfriend.

## 7. Because You Loved Me by Celine Dion

Here's an older song that's always going to be popular. After all, it's sung by the spectacular Celine Dion. It's all about a woman who knows how important the man in her life is. He helped her get through her toughest times, and for that, she's thankful.

## 8. Love Song by Adele

You might've heard the original version of this song, which is by The Cure. However, the cover is just as amazing as the original. Whichever version of the song you listen to, the romantic lyrics will make you melt.

## 9. Grow Old with You by Adam Sandler

Sure, Adam Sandler is a comedian, not a singer. However, that doesn't change the fact that this is one of the most romantic songs you'll ever hear. It's all about a couple who wants to spend the rest of their lives together.

If you can't find a good radio station, then you can slow dance to these sweet songs. Do you and your boyfriend have "a song" of your own?
