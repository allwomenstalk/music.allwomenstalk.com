# 12 Best Summer Songs to Jam out to This Year ...

Music is a great way to make every day better and if you're wondering which are the **best summer songs of 2017**, you have come to the right place. This summer marked the release of some AMAZING songs . These are my choices for the **ultimate summer playlist**.

## 1. BELIEVER- IMAGINE DRAGONS

This song is the perfect kiss-ass song that is a MUST have for the summer. It even features in the finale of the awesome new series Riverdale.

## 2. FETISH- SELENA GOMEZ

This cool and quirky tune will add a funky vibe to your playlist. From the abstract video to unique lyrics, you're going to love this summer song.

## 3. SORRY NOT SORRY- DEMI LOVATO

This might be the most savage song of the summer. It's perfect for the times you want to rock it out. Demi Lovato is the savage, WE BOW DOWN.

## 4. CRYING in the CLUB- CAMILLA CABELLO

Her first official solo song after leaving 5th Harmony is one you don't want to miss. This is guaranteed to give you the feels.

## 5. XO TOUR LLIF3- LIL UZI VERT

This is the KING of savage songs. From the popp'n beat to the fiesty lyrics- this is playlist perfect.

## 6. BACK to YOU- LOUIS TOMLINSOM FT.BEBE REXA & DIGITAL FARM ANIMALS

Tommo's second solo song is as AMAZING (if not more) as his first song. The video is set in his home town of DONCASTER, and includes his childhood buddies.

## 7. PRAYING- KE$HA

This song is all about emotional, empowering goodness. It was inspired by very personal, tough times in her life and it's awesome how she used this traumatizing event to empowerherself and others.

## 8. NO PROMISES- CHEAT CODE FT. DEMI LOVATO

Demi does it once again, this time accompanied with Cheat Code. This carefree song is promised to give you a relaxed summer feel.

## 9. BOYS-CHARLI XCX

This starstudded song is the perfect pick me up for any blue day. It boasts celebrities such as, Cameron Dallas, Joe Jonas, Charlie Puth, Will.i.am, Diplo, Bring me the Horizon, Brendon Urie, Mark Ronson, Stormzy, The Vamps, and Wiz Khalifah- to say the least.

## 10. 2U- DAVID GUETTA FT. JUSTIN BIEBER

This love song is so sweet it won't be a surprise if it's made of angel kisses.This is the perfect song to brighten anyone's day.

## 11. PRETTY GIRLS- MAGGIE LINDERMANN

If there was a Goddess of Girl Power she would DEFINITELY be it. It's bomb-ass lyrics and empowering tone is why this song belongs on every girls playlist.

## 12. GET LOW- LIAM PAYNE & ZEDD

Payno did it again, producing a winning song. This collab screams summer, from the Tumblr worthy video to the upbeat tone. It belongs on ALL Summer playlists.
