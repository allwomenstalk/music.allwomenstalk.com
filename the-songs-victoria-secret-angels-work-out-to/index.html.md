# The 7 Songs Victoria Secret Angels Work out to ️ ...

[Vogue](https://www.vogue.com/article/victorias-secret-show-workout-playlist-angels-lily-aldridge-candice-swanepoel) sat down with the Victoria Secret Angels to find out their favorite songs to exercise to. Here are some of Lily Aldridge, Candice Swanepoel, Sara Sampaio, and Jasmine Tookes' favorite tunes:

## 1. Let’s Go Dancing by Tiga

This is Candice Swanepoel's favorite song for finding her focus.

## 2. Rumors by Jake Miller

This one pumps up Tookes.

## 3. Can't Feel My Face by the Weeknd

Aldridge loves this tune.

## 4. Hotline Bling by Drake

Everybody else loves this song, so it shouldn't be surprising the angels love it too.

## 5. Drops of Jupiter by Train

This is a classic you can never get enough of.

## 6. Break Free by Ariana Grande

Don't you love the beat of it?

## 7. Run the World by Beyonce

This should be every woman's anthem!
