# 10 Upbeat Party Songs for Girls Who Love to Dance ...

Maybe you're getting ready to host a big party. Maybe you just want to turn up the music for you and your friends, so you can dance around in your bedroom together. Either way, it always helps to have a few upbeat songs on your iPod. If you're lacking in that area, here are a few party songs for ladies who love to dance:

## 1. Love Myself by Hailee Steinfeld

This song is not only fun to dance to, but it'll boost your self-esteem. It's all about loving yourself, just like the title suggests. After all, you don't need anybody else in order to feel whole. You can be single and still love life, because you're fine on your own. If you don't believe that, then maybe listening to this song will change your attitude.

## 2. Make Me like You by Gwen Stefani

It doesn't matter if you're in a new relationship or if you're simply crushing on a new guy. Either way, you're going to be able to relate to these adorable lyrics. They're about being mad at a guy for making you like him. It's the way most of us feel whenever we fall for someone new.

## 3. No Control by One Direction

One Direction has had plenty of hits over the years, but this song is one of the best ones to dance to. If you haven't heard it yet, then it's time to put on your headphones and listen. If you own the Wii U, then you can even buy the newest Just Dance game, because this song is included on the disc. That way, you won't have to make up your own dance moves. You'll have dance moves to follow along with on the screen.

## 4. Right Back at It Again by a Day to Remember

If you've never listened to this band before, then you're missing out. They're incredibly talented and are even going out on tour again soon. If you want to hear the actual song, then you should skip ahead to 0:55 ino the video. That's when the music starts up and the real fun begins.

## 5. Bad Girls Club by Falling in Reverse

This song is fun to dance around to with your girlfriends. You're going to love singing along to it after a breakup, especially once you get to 2:35. It'll make you feel like a sexy little cheerleader.

## 6. Man! I Feel like a Woman by Shania Twain

This song is a little bit older, but it's still sounds amazing today. If you want to feel sexy in your skin, then you should listen to it. It's all about embracing your womanhood. After all, you're a gorgeous lady. You might as well enjoy it.

## 7. Shut up by Simple Plan

Here's a song you can dance around to when you're sick of society. That means you don't even have to wait until a party to put this one on. You can blast it when you're alone in your room and want to dance around while singing into your hairbrush. It'll make you feel better after a rough day filled with haters.

## 8. "Single Ladies (Put a Ring on It)," Beyoncé

Newly single and out with your girls? This is a must for every playlist for getting ready and going out on the town.

## 9. "Just Dance," [Lady Gaga](https://www.popsugar.com/Lady-Gaga), Colby O'Donis

The beginning of Lady Gaga's singing career and great memories with your girlfriends--what could be better than that? You'll just wanna dance and turn it up!

## 10. "A Little Party Never Killed Nobody (All We Got)," [Fergie](https://www.popsugar.com/Fergie), Q-Tip, GoonRock

This is the perfect upbeat song to get you off your feet and dance! A little party never killed nobody, right? This song has a very roaring 20's Gatsby feel to it!

Now you'll be able to dance around with your friends whenever you feel like it! What's your favorite party song of all time?
