# 7 Angry Breakup Songs Thatll Help You Express Your Pain ...

Don't fall apart, just because a boy broke your heart. You can get through the pain. You just might need a little help. According to [Shape,](http://www.shape.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) here area some of the best angry breakup songs to help you get out your feelings:

## 1. Mama's Broken Heart

This one is sung by the beautiful Miranda Lambert, who recently split from her hubby Blake Shelton.

## 2. Thnks Fr Th Mmrs

All of you Fall Out Boy fans already know this one.

## 3. Love Drunk

This son is by Boys Like Girls. Remember them?

## 4. Smile

Lily Allen sings this sassy song.

## 5. Really Don't Care

You'll probably recognize this song from Demi Lovato & Cher Lloyd.

## 6. Ex's & Oh's

Elle King has been on the radio a lot recently with this hit.

## 7. Part of Me

You can't forget about this classic Katy Perry song.
