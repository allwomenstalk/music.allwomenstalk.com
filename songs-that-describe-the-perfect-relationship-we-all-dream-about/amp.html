10 Songs That Describe the Perfect Relationship We All Dream about ...

Perfect by One Direction • You Are in Love by Taylor Swift • 8th World Wonder by Kimberly Locke • Until the Day I Die by Story of the Year • A Thousand Miles by Vanessa Carlton • More ...

By Holly

There are plenty of sad songs out there about cheating and breakups. There are just as many songs about abusive relationships. Since they're all pretty depressing, it's time to listen to some music that'll motivate you to find the perfect man. Here are a few songs that describe the perfect relationships that we all dream about having one day:

1. Perfect by One Direction

Sometimes, your version of a perfect relationship isn't having someone you can take home to meet your parents. Maybe you just want to have a little fun with someone nice and sexy. If that's the case, then you're going to love this song. It's all about being in an exciting relationship that'll give you plenty of stories to brag to your friends about.

2. You Are in Love by Taylor Swift

This is one of Taylor's slower songs, but it's one of the best ones from her newest album. It's all about realizing that you're deeply in love with your boyfriend. It's a song about the simple things in a relationship, but isn't that all everyone wants?

3. 8th World Wonder by Kimberly Locke

You might recognize Kimberly from way back in the early seasons of American Idol. This song is about considering your boyfriend to be one of the natural wonders of the universe. It has adorable lyrics like, "I guess that I'm just falling deeper into something I've never known. But the way that I'm feeling makes me realize that it can't be wrong."

4. Until the Day I Die by Story of the Year

This is a song about dating someone that you'd do anything for. Even if your relationship isn't technically "perfect," it'll still feel perfect when you're with the right person. This catchy song has romanic lyrics like, "If you died right now, you know that I'd die too."

5. A Thousand Miles by Vanessa Carlton

You've probably heard this song before, because it used to be incredibly popular. It's all about a girl who would do anything to see her man--even walk across the Earth. She loves him so much that she'd do anything to see him and to touch him.

6. That's How You Know from Enchanted

The movie, Enchanted, does a great job of describing love. There's even an entire song dedicated to it. It explains how a woman can tell when her man is truly in love with her. It has lyrics like, "Does he leave a little note to tell you you are on his mind? Send you yellow flowers when the sky is grey? He'll find a new way to show you a little bit everyday."

7. No One else by Allison Iraheta

This is another star that earned her fame on American Idol. She sings this sweet song about falling in love with her best friend. It has lyrics like, "There's simply no one else in the world I could ever find who likes me when I'm low and still tolerates me when I'm high." Every line is more romantic than the next.

8. Halo by Beyoncé

Oh the song! The one that reminds us of the love that came out of no where after a huge heartbreak and tear down our walls without any hesitation. With lyrics like, "Everywhere I'm looking now, I'm surrounded by your embrace Baby, I can see your halo, You know you're my saving grace" you can't fight it!

9. Teenage Dream by Katy Perry

The love that we all want-the one where we're wanted in our barest state. The one that screams real. With lyrics like, "You think I'm pretty without any makeup on" how can you not help but smile hoping one day you have that?!

10. Just the Way You Are by Bruno Mars

The song girls drooled over forever! With lyrics like, "When I see your face, there's not a thing that I would change, girl you're amazing, just the way you are" It's hard not to fall for him! He's got you feeling all the feels with how smitten he is over that girl and we all want someone to feel that for us.

Don't let all of the depressing songs on the radio fool you into thinking that love never lasts. Listen to positive songs, like these, instead! What other songs do you think capture "the perfect relationship" pretty well?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some songs that describe the perfect relationship?
Oh, there are so many! Have you ever heard of 'All of Me' by John Legend? It's just so heartfelt and talks about loving every part of someone. Another great one is 'Perfect' by Ed Sheeran. It's like the soundtrack to everyone's love story.
Do any songs talk about finding the perfect man?
Yes, absolutely! Bruno Mars' 'Just The Way You Are' is a classic. It’s all about finding someone who loves you for who you are. And then there's 'Love Story' by Taylor Swift. It might be a bit dreamy, but it’s all about finding your perfect match.
Are there songs that highlight understanding in a relationship?
For sure! 'Fix You' by Coldplay is so touching. It really talks about being there for each other through thick and thin. Also, 'A Thousand Years' by Christina Perri is all about patience and understanding in love.
What would be a good song for a perfect couple?
'Marry You' by Bruno Mars is super fun and sweet for couples. And 'Lucky' by Jason Mraz & Colbie Caillat is just adorable! It’s all about feeling lucky to be in love with your best friend.
Has any artist hidden easter eggs in their music videos about relationships?
Totally! Taylor Swift is the queen of easter eggs. There are so many hidden messages in her videos that fans love to decode. 'Lover' is a treasure trove when it comes to little hints about her relationships.
