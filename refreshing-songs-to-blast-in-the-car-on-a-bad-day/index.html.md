# 7 Refreshing Songs to Blast in the Car on a Bad Day ...

When you're having a rough day, there are refreshing songs you can blast in your car or your bedroom. There's no sense in wallowing in your misery. Sometimes, you just have to scream out some lyrics in order to feel better. Here are some refreshing songs to listen to the next time you're angry about a guy, your financial situation, or life in general:

## 1. Crazy Ex Girlfriend by Miranda Lambert

This is one of the refreshing songs that is perfect for whenever you run into an ex. There's nothing wrong with getting a little crazy when you see your old flame with a new girl--especially if she looks like a supermodel. You don't want to start throwing things around, so you can let your emotions loose by singing along to these angry lyrics. If you've ever run into an ex and his new girl, then you already know exactly how Miranda feels.

## 2. Dollhouse by MELANIE Martinez

Sometimes, your family isn't exactly what they seem. This song talks about how easily it is to deceive others. Your house might look like the perfect place to live, but everyone has their secrets.

## 3. Hate (I Really Don't like You) by Plain White T's

Hate is a strong word, so you shouldn't just toss it around. That's what this song is all about. When you're incredibly frustrated with someone, but you don't exactly hate them, then these lyrics are perfect for how you're feeling. You might not know many songs by the Plain White T's, but you've certainly heard of them, and there's a reason for that. They're super talented, and easy to relate to.

## 4. Misery Business by Paramore

Even without the brilliant lyrics, this song is amazing due to the sound. It's catchy, so beware, because it might end up stuck in your head for the rest of the day. If you're having boy problems, then this song will make you feel better. Everyone has issues when it comes to relationships, so don't think that you're alone.

## 5. Break Stuff by Limp Bizkit

There are quite a few bad words in this song, so don't listen if you're against cursing. But if the words don't bother you, you'll end up loving this song. It's perfect for when you're angry at anything and everything. The tune and lyrics fit together well, and will make you feel better about your horrid day.

## 6. Lifestyles of the Rich and Famous by Good Charlotte

Are you annoyed by your empty wallet? Are you frustrated by all of the celebrities that have more cash than they can handle and do nothing productive with their lives? If you are, then you'll love listening to this song. It tells it like it is.

## 7. I Hate Everything about You by Three Days Grace

Have you ever hated someone, but loved them at the same time? It's frustrating, because no matter what they do, they still hold a place in your heart. If you're annoyed by the love of your life, then this is the song for you.

Music is the perfect way to unleash your frustrated feelings. Blast these songs the next time you're having a rough day, and let your worries drift away. What's your favorite angry song to listen to when life goes downhill?
