# 7 Country Love Songs to Play at Your Wedding ...

If you've been looking for music to play at your wedding, you should consider some country love songs. Some people know exactly what songs they want played at their wedding the first time they hear them, but others need a little time to listen to different ones. Whether you need music for your first dance or music for your guests to dance to at your wedding reception, there's tons of great country love songs to choose from. Here's 7 of my top choices.

## 1. Lost in This Moment by Big & Rich

After its release, this song quickly became one of the most played country love songs. This song is sung by country music duo Big & Rich and tells the story of a couple's wedding from the groom's point of view. This song shows us that even though there is so much work and planning involved in a wedding, the most important thing is the perfect moments shared between the couple getting married.

## 2. Bless the Broken Road by Rascal Flatts

There are several different songs by Rascal Flatts that are perfect for weddings, but this is my favorite. It shows us that life is not always perfect and sometimes it takes a while to find the one. Sometimes we enter into relationships that we think are going to work, but for some reason they don't. It can also seem like you're never going to find the right one and then one day, there they are. This song is about those struggles and how thankful you are when you know you've found the right one.

## 3. Amazed by Lonestar

This is such a beautiful song. It's easy to see why it's one of the most popular country wedding songs of all time. My sister choose this one for the first dance at her wedding and it was a perfect choice. This song is about loving every little thing about your significant other and how truly amazing they are.

## 4. It's Your Love by Tim McGraw Featuring Faith Hill

Many couples consider this song as “their song”. This heartfelt ballad is sung by country music power couple Tim McGraw and Faith Hill and they did it beautifully. I can't image it sung by anyone else. “It's Your Love” is about all of the things that you love about your significant other and how you're better because of them. This tune is perfect for your first dance or reception.

## 5. I Swear by John Michael Montgomery

This song has been recorded by both country singer John Micheal Montgomery and the band All-4-One. "I Swear" is a song about the promises made between two people vowing to love one another no matter what. This widely popular song has also been featured on television shows and in movies.

## 6. What a Beautiful Day by Chris Cagle

If you're looking for something a little more upbeat to play at your wedding reception, this is a good choice. This song tells the story of a couple's relationship. It starts from when they meet and takes you up to their 50th wedding anniversary. This one is great for your wedding; guests can enjoy dancing to it or just listen to the words.

## 7. It's a Love Thing by Keith Urban

This song is sung by Keith Urban who is knows a thing or two about love, since he's married to the lovely Nicole Kidman. "It's A Love Thing" is an upbeat fun song that's also a great choice to play at your wedding reception. In this song Keith sings about being wrapped around his lady's finger and how he doesn't care what everyone else thinks about it.

I hope you enjoyed my list of country wedding songs. Do you have a favorite wedding song that you would like to add to the list? I can't wait to hear what your favorite are.
