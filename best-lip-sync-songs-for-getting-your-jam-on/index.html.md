# 12 Best Lip Sync Songs for Getting Your Jam on ...

It all starts at a young age; us girls singing in our rooms into our hair brush and dancing around. Then it turns into jamming out in the car with your BFFs. But what are the best songs to lip sync to? What songs really let you belt it out without actually having to let your voice show? Well, here's the inside scoop below.

## 1. Bohemian Rhapsody-Queen

I am the biggest queen fan ever! So of course this had to be on the list. The different vocal ranges throughout the song allow you to really give it your all during a lip sync concert without having to try to match pitch-because let's be real, no one can compete with Freddie!

## 2. Call Me Maybe-Carly Rae Jepsen

Remember a few years ago this song was all the rage on the radio and among those online remaking the video? Well, if you're looking for a throwback and to lip sync your heart out-this is it! It's funny, it's easy to get overly dramatic with, and it's actually not a bad song to listen to.

## 3. Respect-Aretha Franklin

R-E-S-P-E-C-T! Yes, that song. The [queen of soul music](https://music.allwomenstalk.com/powerful-and-anthemic-songs-from-queens-of-soul/) probably never expected her song to span generations but seriously, who hasn't been disrespected in a relationship?! Coming off a breakup, this song will be awesome for letting your feelings out.

## 4. Single Ladies-Beyonce

Oh yeah! This song of course had to go on the list! Everyone knows the moves (sorta) and it's always great to show appreciation for the Queen Bey. This song is awesome by itself but then throw in your lip sync concert and you've got a hit on your hands.

## 5. Don't Stop Believing-Journey

Would it be a song list if it didn't have Journey on it?! This song is great for rocking out, karoke, or lip syncing. When this song comes on, I don't care what race, religion, color, gender, sexual orientation, species you are... EVERYONE is a small town girl living in a lonely world.

## 6. Hound Dog-Elvis

No one can match the extraordinary voice that was Elvis Presley. His music has lived beyond his years and there is no way you can't have a great time lip syncing to any Elvis song. You and your friends will enjoy Hound Dog, Jailhouse Rock, and more by the King.

## 7. Achy Breaky Heart-Billy Ray Cyrus

The country music star had a hit on his hands when he released Achy Breaky Heart. It's funny, cheesy, and definitely one you'll have a great time lip syncing to. Whether you're in the car, hanging with friends, or by yourself, you'll love this song.

## 8. I Will Always Love You-Whitney Houston

Another voice that is impossible to match. Whitney Houston was a one of a kind voice, but that doesn't mean you can't have fun pretending to sing like her by lip syncing one of her greatest hits. This song pulls at your heart strings and definitely is a great way to let out those feelings you've been holding in post heart break.

## 9. Old Time Rock and Roll-Bob Segar

This song is a classic for those in the lip sync world. Tom Cruise first made lip syncing to this song cool in his movie Risky Business in which he slid through a house in a button down shirt, underwear, and socks. YouTube the scene then make sure you jump on the bandwagon and lip sync this song for yourself!

## 10. Wrecking Ball-Miley Cyrus

Oh this song! Wonderful. Wonderful. Wonderful. Make this song on your top lip sync songs. Everyone knows it for the iconic music video and it's bound to be a great jam session with you and your friends.

## 11. Any Disney Song Ever!

Yes, I'm talking Disney movies. Those songs are my lip sync jam. It brings back my childhood and I get to have fun with my kids all at the same time. You probably know quite a few of them from "Let It Go" to "Kiss the Girl" and right down to the greatest of all to lip sync- Mulan's "I'll Make a Man Out of You".

## 12. Uptown Funk-Bruno Mars

This song used to be on the radio what felt like 24/7 and for good reason. It's upbeat and sure to put a pep in your lip syncing step. Give it a try! You won't be disappointed.

What songs are your favorite to lip sync to? Do you ever lip sync to the ones on this list? Let me know in the comments!
