# 7 Songs to Listen to when You're Missing Someone ...

No matter how happy you are, there's always someone that you miss having in your life. Maybe it's a parent that passed away. Maybe it's a boyfriend that broke up with you. Maybe it's a friend you lost touch with. It doesn't matter who they are, because the feeling is never a pleasant one. If you need help dealing with your emotions, here are a few songs about missing a person you love:

## 1. Where’d You Go by Fort Minor

It you think that rap music is always littered with curses and crude comments, this song will prove you wrong. It's all about a man who's stuck alone, wondering where the person he misses has gone. If it feels like it's been forever since you've seen the person on your mind, this song should help you cope with what you're feeling.

## 2. Wish You Were Here by Avril Lavigne

Avril has always done a great job of describing difficult situations, and this time is no different. In this song, she talks about how much she misses a special someone. She misses the things they said and the things they did--and more than anything, she just wishes that they were there with her.

## 3. I Miss You by Miley Cyrus

This is an old song of Miley's about missing someone near and dear. If you're mourning someone who has passed away, then you should feel comforted by this song. The lyrics are touching, so they just might make you cry.

## 4. I Miss You by Blink 182

Sometimes, you're at fault for people leaving your life. If you did something to push your friends away, you'll enjoy this song with lyrics like, "Where are you? And I'm so sorry. I cannot sleep, I cannot dream tonight."

## 5. Miss You by Enrique Iglesias

This is the perfect song to listen to after a breakup. It's about a man who's feeling lonely and wants his ex in the room, so that he can tell her how much he misses her. It might not be a good idea for you to actually tell your ex how you feel, but this song will be there to comfort you.

## 6. I Miss You like Crazy by Natalie Cole

This is another song that you should listen to after a bad breakup. It has lyrics like, "I tried to deny it, but I'm still in love with you. I miss you like crazy." If you're trying to deal with the way you feel about your ex, songs like these should help you figure out what your best move is.

## 7. Who Knew by Pink

Sometimes, the people you assumed would always be around actually end up leaving your life. If that's how the situation went down for you, you should enjoy this song. It has lyrics like, "If someone said three years from now, you'd be long gone, I'd stand up and punch them out, because they're all wrong."

If you miss someone that you're able to pick up the phone and call, don't hesitate to do so. Of course, just because you're missing them doesn't mean they should be in your life. If they hurt you, it's okay to keep them in your past. What's your favorite song about missing someone?
