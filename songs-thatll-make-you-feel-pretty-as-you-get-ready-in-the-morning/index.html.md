# 10 Songs That'll Make You Feel Pretty as You Get Ready in the Morning ...

When you get ready in the morning, you should put on some music. That way, you'll get pumped up and can start your day off on the right foot. Of course, to do that, you need some fitting songs. If you listen to these while you're getting dressed and doing your makeup, you're bound to feel beautiful:

## 1. Good for You by Selena Gomez

Most girls claim that they don't dress for a man, because they only care about themselves. Of course, if you want to look good to impress your crush, there's nothing wrong with that. Selena proves that in this song about wanting to look good for her man. If you want to make him drool over you, go for it.

## 2. Try by Colbie Caillat

If you're not in the mood to cover your skin with makeup and slip into a tight little dress, listen to this song. It'll remind you that your looks aren't everything. You're beautiful just the way you are. You don't need to put in extra effort in order to look great, because you're already perfect.

## 3. Annie by Safetysuit

This song is about a woman who's beautiful, but doesn't realize it yet. That describes pretty much every one of us, don't you think? After all, we're all gorgeous, but it's hard for us to see that, even if others compliment us on a daily basis. That's why you should listen to the lyrics in this song and realize that looks don't make the world go 'round--and even if they did, it wouldn't matter, because you're beautiful.

## 4. One in a Million by the Backstreet Boys

This song is about a man who thinks that an "average" woman is beyond beautiful. Even though she's stuck at a dead-end job and her makeup is always smeared, he still finds her gorgeous and wishes that she could see how amazing she really is.

## 5. Lip Gloss by Lil Mama

Even though the lyrics are pretty silly, this is a catchy song you'll be stuck singing all day. If you love your lipgloss, or any of your other makeup products, you'll understand why it could put the singer in such a good mood. Hopefully, it'll put you in a great mood as well, so that you can go through your day with a smile on your face and a spring in your step.

## 6. Confident by Demi Lovato

Demi is the queen of confidence. She always releases relatable music that is meant to inspire women everywhere, and this song is no different. You've probably heard it already, because it's gaining popularity by the day, but that doesn't mean you can't listen to it again. Make it your anthem.

## 7. Conceited by Remy Ma

There's nothing wrong with thinking that you're the hottest thing in the room. In fact, it's actually healthy to think that way. Instead of focusing on your flaws, you should be focusing on your positive qualities. This song will motivate you to start thinking highly of yourself. After all, you deserve the very best.

## 8. Independent Women by Destiny's Child

There is no better anthem for women than Destiny's Child - Independent Women. It highlights the sheer awesomeness that is being a woman and inspires us to feel confident, sexy and independent.

## 9. Born This Way by Lady Gaga

This track is perfect for anyone doubting who or what they are. Lady Gaga makes us all feel special by encouraging us to feel amazing just as we are. And what are we? Beautiful!

## 10. What Makes You Beautiful by One Direction

This song will not only make you feel stunning but will also give you a bit of eye candy before you start your day - a day that you're going to rock, by the way.

You're beautiful, but you might not realize that until singers push you to see it. What songs do you usually listen to in the morning as you're getting ready to start your day?
