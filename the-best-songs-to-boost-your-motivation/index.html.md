# The Best Songs to Boost Your Motivation for Girls Procrastinating

Are you looking for **songs to boost your motivation?** We all need a kick in the butt every now and then, and this playlist will be all you need! Blast the jams and get your booty shaking while you work. Crank it up with the best playlist for getting you going by adding these **songs to boost your motivation** to your playslist.

## 1. Hells Bells - AC/DC

Nothing to kick-start your day like a little AC/DC, right? This is one of the best **songs to boost your motivation.**

## 2. I Don’t Wanna Be - Gavin DeGraw

Need some inspiration to be happy with yourself? Look no further.

## 3. Believer - Imagine Dragons

This is a great song for starting something new.

## 4. Centuries - Fall out Boy

You’re a legend, and this song reminds you of it.

## 5. Not Afraid - Eminem

This song basically tells all the haters to go you-know-what themselves.

## 6. Warriors - Imagine Dragons

”You always knew you’d be the one to work while they all play.”

## 7. Scars to Your Beautiful - Alessia Cara

This song reminds you to feel good about yourself, and the beat keeps you going.

## 8. The Nights - Avicii

Live it up, live for the moment, “Live a life you will remember.” This world is ours to own, and Avicii reminds us of that.

## 9. ‘Till I Collapse - Eminem

Basically, Eminem is telling us to get up off our asses and do some great things because we know we can!

## 10. Wake Me up - Avicii

It may be very “2013,” but this anthem is one that should never be forgotten, not just because it was an incredible hit, but because it’s message is beautiful.

## 11. Paradise - Coldplay

The timeless alternative rock group gives us a song about yearning to be in a beautiful place that perhaps is only inside our minds - it's called paradise. It’s a great song for running away from your problems for a minute and can really get you thinking.

## 12. Move along - All-American Rejects

Because sometimes, all we really can do is just, "Move Along."
