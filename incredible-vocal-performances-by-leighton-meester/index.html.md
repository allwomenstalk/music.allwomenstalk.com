# 7 Incredible Vocal Performances by Leighton Meester ...

There are so many performances by Leighton Meester that are very, very impressive. I always knew she dabbled a bit in music while acting, but I never knew how amazing her vocals really were. When I started listening to her music, I was blown away. It's so different that the pop stuff that she first came out with. She's a magnificent songwriter and I love the direction she's gone in. I can't wait for an official album or even a tour! Check out these amazing performances by Leighton Meester.

## 1. Heart Strings

This tops my list for amazing performances by Leighton Meester. I mean, must I explain why? I never even knew Leighton had this vocal range! I still watch and listen to this video daily. Unfortunately, I haven't been able to find a studio version of this song, but I assume it doesn't get much better than this. It's perfect.

## 2. Entitled

I'm totally obsessed with this song, too. The lyrics are beautiful and her voice sounds amazing. This is the type of music Leighton should be making/releasing. You can tell that this style is really her, it's just effortless. I love that this performance features Check in the Dark because they're pretty epic too.

## 3. Blue Afternoon

Can I just start off by saying how much I love her outfit? She's so adorable! I love this performance of "Blue Afternoon". She looks so comfortable on stage, which makes me so happy when I watch these videos. I can't help but still think of Leighton as Blair Waldorf (am I the only one?). This Queen B definitely has some pipes on her.

## 4. The Stand in

When I first heard this song, I fell in love with it. The lyrics are so beautiful! I had this video on repeat for so long and still play it back once in a while. Leighton's voice paired with Mike Frieman's is magical. Check in the Dark wrote this song and Leighton sounds great on it. I wish I had been in the audience for this. This was back in 2011 and I'm still hoping they'll get together sometime soon and perform it again. I know I'd be the first in line!

## 5. On My Side

Oh my gosh, her dancing! I'm sorry, I'm having major Leighton feels right now. I love this song, I think she sounds great. The lyrics are fantastic and I love that the crowd is so into it. This song has such a cool and mellow vibe to it.

## 6. Give in to Me

Every song on the County Strong soundtrack is gold. This one, however, is my absolute favorite. Leighton and Garrett sound incredible together. Unfortunately, I couldn't find any live recordings of this, but this will do! This is such a beautiful song. If this is the first time you're hearing this song, I'm sure you'll love it just as much as I do!

## 7. Jenny

Here's another great video that features Check in the Dark. The audio in this video isn't the greatest, but you can still hear Leighton's vocals and that's what counts. I love how lost in the music they get and they put on such a great show.

There you have it! These seven performances by Leighton Meester are incredible and never cease to amaze me. I sincerely hope Leighton makes more music like this. Which one is your favorite? Will you be catching her on Broadway this year?
