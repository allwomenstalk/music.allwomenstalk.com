# 7 Body Positive Songs That Will Make You Feel Gorgeous ...

Listening to body positive songs is a great way to start feeling more comfortable in your own skin. There are way too many magazines and TV shows out there that make it seem like all women should look a certain way, but that's not true at all. You're beautiful, no matter what the media tries to tell you. Here are some catchy body positive songs that will help you realize that you're beautiful just the way you are:

## 1. Try by Colbie Caillat

Do you spend hours in front of the mirror before you leave the house, making sure to cover up every single flaw on your face? Well, this is one of the body positive songs that will convince you that you don't have to try to make everyone love you by making yourself look conventionally pretty. Colbie's video shows women just like you without make-up and with their natural hair, but they look just as gorgeous as ever. Be like them, and don't be afraid to step out of the house without fixing yourself up first.

## 2. Imperfect is the New Perfect by Caitlin Crosby

What's the appeal of being 'perfect?' Wouldn't you rather be real? Every single human in this world has flaws, so stop comparing yourself to women in magazines who won't even look like their photographs when you see them in real life. Embrace your imperfections, because it's what makes you you.

## 3. All about That Bass by Meghan Trainor

You've probably heard this song by now, because it's dominating the radio. If you're self-conscious about your weight, then this song can change your outlook on your image. It doesn't matter what the scale says, because you're a gorgeous girl who can rock her body.

## 4. Unpretty by TLC

Everyone has felt unpretty before. We all have our own insecurities that haunt us, but we can't let them control us. You have to love yourself, and ignore anyone who criticizes your appearance. They aren't worth your time, because your body only deserves affection.

## 5. The Beauty in Ugly by Jason Mraz

There's nothing wrong with looking like an 'ordinary' girl instead of a beauty queen. You're still a gorgeous person. Like Jason says, you're one of a kind, and that makes you beautiful, so stand up tall.

## 6. Perfect by Pink

You're a valuable person, and you deserve to know that. If the people in your life treat you like you're worthless, don't let them get to you. You're amazing, no matter what they say to you or do to you. It doesn't matter if they see how perfect you are, as long as you know it yourself.

## 7. I Love Myself Today by Bif Naked

Don't let a breakup make you feel like you're any less beautiful than you are. You should love yourself, even if it feels like nobody else does. Instead of avoiding the mirror, learn to enjoy walking up to it to catch a glimpse of the remarkable woman reflected in it.

Don't let your insecurities stop you from living a happy life. It's normal to feel like you're 'ugly,' but you can't continue to keep that mindset. You have to pay more attention to your special traits than to your flaws, and learn to embrace the body that you have. What's your favorite thing about your body?
