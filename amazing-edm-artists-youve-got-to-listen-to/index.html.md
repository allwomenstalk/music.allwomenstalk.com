# 7 Amazing Edm Artists You've Got to Listen to ...

Electronic Dance Music has taken over the music world and there are few EDM artists that you have to know about. EDM is a genre that gets you pumped, which is perfect for any occasion or mood you're in. Here is a list of some EDM artists that will blow your mind!

## 1. Avicii

![Avicii](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tl/n1/pa/npalpx7z_600x393.jpg)

I'm sure you've heard of Avicii, one of the greatest of EDM artists at the moment! Avicii, also known as 24 year old Tim Berg, took the world by storm with well known songs, "Wake Me Up," "Levels," "Silhouettes," and the current hit, "Hey Brother." One of my favorite songs by him that you may have heard is called "Seek Bromance." This was actually the first song I've ever heard by Avicii!

## 2. Klaypex

![Klaypex](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0w/bb/wp/yhfgbl4t_600x400.jpg)

Klaypex are incredible EDM artists consisting of two 24 year-olds, Johnny Atar and Mark Emmanuel, based in Miami, FL. Transitioning from a rock band, to a pop band, and finally to EDM artists, these two have never lost their love for music! You may have heard their song "Lights" that has blown up on YouTube. My personal favorite by Klaypex is called "I Walk Alone." From amazing original songs to covers and remixes, Klaypex always satisfies! They are a must in your workout playlist!

## 3. Deadmau5

![Deadmau5](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/vf/b6/pqs2uokc_600x400.jpg)

Deadmau5 is a 33 year old Canadian EDM artist, otherwise known as Joel Zimmerman. He has been in the music industry for over a decade, creating a very interesting vibe of EDM music. His music ranges from different vibes, from very relaxing songs, to songs that really get you pumped. You may have heard songs like, "Ghosts n' Stuff," "I Remember," or "Strobe." He is an amazing EDM artist that you need in your playlist!

## 4. David Guetta

![David Guetta](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/na/j8/drm4q1h1_600x439.jpg)

You have definitely heard of David Guetta from creating many of your favorite hits and working with many of the well known singers of today. To name a few of his countless hits, we've got "Play Hard," "Titanium," and "She Wolf." Sound familiar? He has worked with artists including Kelly Rowland, Chris Brown, Rihanna, Lady Gaga, Usher, and countless of other pop and even rap artists! He is bound to be on your playlist!

## 5. The Glitch Mob

![The Glitch Mob](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/qc/vt/x2svcowo_600x400.jpg)

The Glitch Mob is a group of three members, Edward Ma, Justin Boreta, and Josh Mayer. I can assure you that their music is like nothing you've ever heard! This is the type of music to blast in your car, in your room, and at the gym. My favorite tracks include "We Can Make the World Stop," "Drive It Like You Stole It," and "Warrior Concerto."

## 6. Martin Garrix

![Martin Garrix](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/zd/mf/lopuqd0q_600x400.jpg)

Martin Garrix, who is only 17 years old, is an up and coming EDM artist who has taken over with the famous track "Animals" that has not stopped playing on the radio! He has recently released a new track, "Wizard," which has already gone viral. With Martin Garrix's twist on EDM music, he is bound to make history in the EDM world! This is a must artist on your playlist.

## 7. Nero

![Nero](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/v0/y1/o6f8isxb_600x399.jpg)

Nero is a British EDM band consisting of three members, Daniel Stephens, Joe Ray, and Alana Watson. They are known for working with artists such as Skrillex, Chase & Status, Muse, and Flux Pavilion. Some well known songs by Nero include "Promises," "Innocence," which is actually one of the first few songs to introduce me to Dubstep music, and "Act Like You Know."

These EDM artists all have one thing in common - amazing music! I hope you guys give them a listen and see exactly what I'm talking about. What are some of your favorite EDM artists?
