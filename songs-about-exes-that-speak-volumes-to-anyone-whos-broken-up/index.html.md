# Songs about Exes That Speak Volumes to Anyone Whos Broken up ...

Breakups are a part of life. We all go through at least one at some point. Luckily, there are plenty of songs dealing with the same topic that can help us move on and feel less alone. These are just a few of the ones we love to belt out.

## 1. Shout out to My Ex by Little Mix🔊

The title of this recent release by Little Mix is enough to make it the perfect addition to this list, combined with lyrics that throw the perfect amount of shade at your ex.

## 2. So What by Pink

Pink belts out this tune about moving on in her classic wild fashion. She reminds her ex that yes, she is a rock star 🌟 and he’s a tool. Perfect.

## 3. Since U Been Gone by Kelly Clarkson

“Since you’ve been gone, I can breathe for the first time,” Clarkson hits the mark with this classic breakup song that we all love to sing along with.

## 4. We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together by Taylor Swift🚫

The list wouldn’t be complete with a Taylor Swift break up hit. She has plenty, but we like the quirky, sarcastic lyrics.

## 5. Somebody That I Used to Know by Gotye (feat. Kimbra)

The look at both perspectives after a breakup is why we love this song so much. Both Gotye and Kimbra’s voices mesh so well to tell a story of heartbreak.

## 6. Survivor by Destiny’s Child

This is the ultimate power song for anyone after a break up. Destiny’s Child brings it. They’re moving on, and they’re working harder and stronger 💪without that man weighing them down.

## 7. Forget You by Cee Lo Green👋

You really can’t help but sing along to this catchy tune by Cee Lo. “I pity the fool the falls in love with you,” is probably our favorite line.

## 8. Love Yourself by Justin Bieber

Justin’s Love Yourself delivers a great blow to any ex and displays an uncaring attitude in a track from his latest album. It’s a somewhat bitter goodbye to someone he’s better off without.

## 9. Someone like You by Adele

The list wouldn’t be complete with an emotional Adele track, and we chose Someone Like You because it’s so raw. It describes the feelings and thoughts after a breakup perfectly.

## 10. Cry Me a River 😪 by Justin Timberlake

This is the ultimate power anthem for anyone who’s had a messy breakup. Ex did you wrong?? Turn this track up and sing it out, babe.

These are just a few tracks that anyone dealing with a breakup can enjoy. Have any others that didn't make this list? Leave them in the comments.
