# Your Ultimate Summer Workout Playlist so You Can Sweat to the Best Beats ...

A great summer workout playlist can really help on those days where
we have to force ourselves to get up and work out - especially on a hot day! Sometimes,
all it takes is a killer song or group of songs to get our booty shaking and
hit the gym (or pavement for a run or even just our living room). Here's a
great summer workout playlist thanks to laurenconrad.com to get sweating to the
beat and knocking out one of your best workouts yet!
