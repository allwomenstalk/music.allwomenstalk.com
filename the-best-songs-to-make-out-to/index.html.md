# The 9 Best Songs to Make out to ...

When you get your man alone, you need to set the mood to urge him to kiss you. The right music will make you feel like you're in a romance film, and there's nothing sweeter than that. You never know, one of these titles might even become "your song." If you need some tunes to add to your playlist, here are some of the best ones to play when you're alone with your crush:

## 1. Love Song by the Cure

Don't you love being left alone with your man? It makes you feel happier than you've ever been, like you're finally alive. This song will let your man know how great he makes you feel, while reminding him that you'll always love him.

## 2. All of My Heart by Sleeping with Sirens

Have you loved your man for years and years? Then you need to put this song on for him. It'll let him know that you still love him with all of your heart, despite everything you've been through.

## 3. You Are Beautiful by Escape the Fate

If you're sick of slow songs, listen to this one. It'll let your man know that you think he's a beautiful person. He's like no one else, which is why you saved this special song for when you two were alone together.

## 4. If I Never See Your Face Again by Maroon 5 and Rihanna

You should be happy about spending time with your man today, even if you never get to see his face again. That's what living in the moment is all about. It lets you appreciate the present without worrying about what the future has in store for you.

## 5. Fire by the Cast of Glee

Glee may be over, but you don't have to stop listening to the music from the show. This one, sung by Matthew Morrison and Kristin Chenoweth, is about as sweet as it gets. If it doesn't lead to a kiss, nothing will.

## 6. Lost in You by Three Days Grace

If you hate slow songs, here's another one for you. Even though it has an edge to it, it contains some pretty sweet lyrics. Listen to it to hear it for yourself.

## 7. Red Light by the Strokes

When you're with your partner, nothing else matters. After all, two can be complete without the rest of the world. You don't need anybody else or anything else, because you have love.

## 8. No One else by Allison Iraheta

You might remember Allison from American Idol. Even though she came in fourth place, she has the talent of a winner. Plus, she sings about stories we can all relate to. This song in particular is about a girl who has found a guy that's like no one she's ever met.

## 9. When You Say You Love Me by Josh Groban

When you're in the mood for romantic music, you don't have to look any further than Josh Groban. Everything he sings sounds sweet, despite what he's actually saying. This is one of his most famous songs, for good reason. It'll make you and your partner feel like you're the only ones on Earth.

Playing music while you make out will make the experience so much sweeter. Do you and your boyfriend have a song?
