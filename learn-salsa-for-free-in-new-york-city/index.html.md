# The Perfect Way to Learn Salsa Dancing in NYC ...

Did you know that you can **learn salsa for free in New York City**? Talia Castro-Pozo & LatinParty.com presents Latin Mondays at Taj. It was voted one of NYC’s best dance parties by [timeout.com](https://www.timeout.com/newyork)

Maybe you are wondering why it has received rave reviews? Well, you can enjoy an excellent dinner and dance experience at one venue. Partake of [gourmet dishes](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/easy-gourmet-dinners-for-tonight/) from the late night kitchen. And hone your dance skills by taking advantage of the free weekly salsa lesson that starts at 7 pm sharp. It is a lot of fun and good exercise! The class is followed by live music, DJ's and shows. This night spot’s atmosphere is inviting with opulent imported décor such as carved teak, woodwork, sandstone dancing shivas, and a stunning mahogany and copper-topped bar. The medium-sized dance floor is surrounded by seating for socializing at booths, lounges, and the bar. And occasionally you can catch an exciting live dance performance. Latin Mondays at Taj is a great place to meet old friends or make new ones. The crowd is attractive, friendly and ready to dance the night away. Join them at 48 West 21st Street (between 5th & 6th Avenues) downtown Manhattan. You won’t regret taking the time to **learn salsa for free in New York City**!
