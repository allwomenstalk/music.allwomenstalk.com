# 7 Reasons You Should See Demi Lovato in Concert ...

It’s been two years since I saw Demi Lovato in concert and I can’t wait to see her perform live again (tomorrow, as I write this)! I’m a big fan so seeing her perform in concert is always fun. The first time I saw Demi Lovato in concert, my family joined me and they couldn’t stop raving about how great she is live. Now that she’s on her Neon Lights Tour, you should definitely snag a ticket and get out to see her amazing show!

## 1. Awesome Vocals

Demi Lovato has an amazing voice. I was very impressed seeing her in concert because she sounded just as good live as she does on her CDs. I can’t stand seeing bands in concert that sound nothing like their soundtracks. To me, an authentic voice is more valuable when you're putting down money to buy a ticket than wasting money on a less-than-lovely live performance. But don’t just take my word for it, see Demi Lovato in concert for yourself and you won’t be disappointed!

## 2. Between Songs

Demi is very down-to-earth, which is something I really admire about her. Between her songs she talks to the audience. And I mean she actually talks. Whether it’s a shout-out about mental health or the healing power of music, I like that she takes the time to say something. In an interview, she recently said that she enjoys talking with her fans during the show because they mean the world to her.

## 3. Involved

Demi gets involved in all her songs during her performance. Her dances are choreographed beautifully. Her stage presence is very commanding. You are bound to leave her concert satisfied by the amount of time and energy Demi puts in to making her concerts great.

## 4. Passion

Her passion for singing inspires me. It’s refreshing to see her perform because she’s passionate about doing it. I find it great that Demi can do something that she loves. I love the spark that shines in your eyes when you are doing something you love. That feeling coats the air at a Demi Lovato concert. The visual effects are so cool, you can’t help but fall in love with the work Demi put in.

## 5. Good Songs

Demi’s songs are full of great lyrics and awesome beats. Seeing her in concert is worthwhile because her fast-beat songs make the concert great. When I saw her live two years ago, I couldn’t have been more pleased with the performance. I’m seeing her again tomorrow and I am so excited! An artist that can get you this revved up about their concert is definitely worth their salt.

## 6. Concerts

When people get together to enjoy music, you get an amazing vibe. Concerts are fun because you can go with friends and make a night of it. My sister and I are going to the concert together and it’s a great way to bond over music we both love. The crowd at Demi’s concerts really wants to be there and loves the music as much as you do. So if you are looking to meet more Demi fans or hear how great she performs live, then get yourself to a concert soon!

## 7. It’s Demi

Demi Lovato is an awesome person because not only does she care about her fans and her music, but she puts herself out there to talk about issues that matter. Whether it’s in concert or in interviews, she’s authentic. There is no difference between stage Demi and real life Demi because she acts the way she is. You should see what that looks like live.

Demi Lovato holds amazing concerts that you should definitely get yourself out to see. What is your favorite song by Demi? Have you gotten your tickets for her Neon Lights Tour yet?
