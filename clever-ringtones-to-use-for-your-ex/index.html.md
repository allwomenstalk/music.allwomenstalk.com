# 7 Clever Ringtones to Use for Your Ex ...

If you’re not on good terms with your last boyfriend, there are ringtones to use for your ex that will make you remember not to pick up if he ever calls. You don’t want to get back together with someone when you know they’re wrong for you. As silly as it is, these ringtones are a way to give you the strength to avoid that phone call. These ringtones to use for your ex can be helpful, or just a way to make you laugh when he crosses your mind.

## 1. Telephone

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Lady Gaga sings this song about having too much fun on her own to answer her phone. She sings, “Sorry, I can not hear you. I’m kinda busy.” When your ex calls, remember how happy you can be without him in your life. This is one of the ringtones to use for your ex if you want to enjoy the single life. You don’t need a man to have fun; You can make it happen on your own.

## 2. We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This Taylor Swift song is self explanatory. If you don’t want to get back together with your ex, this song will remind you to keep away. It’s unhealthy to break up and make up constantly. If he’s not the right guy for you, then you need to find the strength to move on. You can’t keep returning to him because he’s familiar.

## 3. Gives You Hell

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This song by the All American Rejects is all about succeeding in life. Once your relationship ends, you‘ll want your ex to know how well you‘re doing without him. Tyson Ritter sings, “And truth be told, I miss you. And truth be told, I’m lying.” This song shows that it’s possible to prove everyone wrong who doesn’t think you’re good enough.

## 4. Look so Good without You

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Jessie James sings this song about moving on after a breakup. She talks about how good it is to be single and how she finally feels like herself again. Everything seems brighter since her relationship ended. She shows that breakups aren’t always a bad thing. Sometimes, they’re exactly what you need.

## 5. Forget You

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Did things leave off badly with your ex? Then this song might be the one for you. Everyone knows this Cee Lo Green tune. While the clean version is good, you’ll probably get more enjoyment out of the uncensored version. Even if the rest of the song doesn’t relate to your situation, you can still sing the chorus.

## 6. Wake up Call

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Maroon 5 sings this song about catching his girlfriend cheating. While the song is a bit dark, it might relate to your situation. If you were with a guy that saw other girls behind your back, you’ll want to give this song a listen. Just don’t do what Adam does in the music video and resort to violence. Release your anger in a healthier way.

## 7. Since You’ve Been Gone

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This song, by Kelly Clarkson, is famous for its relatable lyrics and catchy tune. Instead of answering your ex’s call, you’ll end up singing along to the song. Kelly sings about moving on after a breakup and how her life has improved since the relationship ended. If you’re like her, you’re better off alone than with your ex. Enjoy your single life!

If you’re worried you might pick up a phone call from an ex, assign one of these ringtones to his number. Check out _techhive.com_ for tips on how to convert your music into ringtones. Can you think of any other songs to add to this list?
