# 7 Best Songs to Listen to While Running on the Treadmill ...

You need to listen to encouraging music when you work out so that you don't give up on your goals easily. Hearing the following songs will keep your mind off of your pain and your mind on your end goal, which is to get healthier. You don't want to be too weak to climb a flight of stairs, so you should work out whenever you get the opportunity to do so. Here are some of the best songs to listen to while you're running on the treadmill if you want to keep up your speed:

## 1. Remember the Name by Fort Minor

It's impossible to listen to this song without getting inspired to become stronger. Even if you don't enjoy rap, you'll be pushed to your limits if you pay close attention to the lyrics. Even if you can't understand what is being said, the beat will get your heart pumping. This is one of the best songs to listen to while on the treadmill, because it has a great beat and even better lyrics.

## 2. Here It Goes Again by OK Go

Even the music video for this song will encourage you to exercise. It shows the band members dancing around on treadmills. You don't want to try their routine while you're out the gym, because you'll end up in trouble, but you should try to be as energetic as they are. You'll get fit in no time.

## 3. Stronger by Kelly Clarkson

Even though this song isn't about physical strength, you can pretend that it is once the chorus begins. It'll remind you that what doesn't kill you will only make you stronger. That means you need to push past the pain you're feeling on that treadmill and keep running until you can't run anymore. Let Kelly encourage you to do your best.

## 4. Hero by Skillet

This is one of those songs that has an amazing beat that'll make you feel like you're ready to take on the world. Plus, the lyrics are all about needing a hero. That means that you can pretend that you're Black Widow or Gamora and are going to save the world with your awesomeness.

## 5. Undefeated by Jason Derulo

Jason Derulo always creates catchy songs. This one is sure to get stuck in your head, but it'll also get you moving around. You won't feel like stopping to take a breath when his voice is in your ear. He's surprisingly encouraging.

## 6. Till I Collapse by Eminem

Rap isn't always about disrespecting women and cursing. Sometimes, it's meant to encourage you. Eminem has quite a few songs that will inspire you to become a better person. This one is the best of all for working out, because it tells you to keep trying until you collapse.

## 7. Eye of the Tiger by Survivor

Here's the classic that you were waiting for. If you really need a push to keep running, you need to listen to this or any of the other songs from the soundtrack of the Rocky movies. They're meant to make you move, so they won't disappoint.

Music is magical, because it can completely alter your mood. If you're feeling lazy, put on one of these songs and get ready to move. What songs do you usually listen to while you're running on the treadmill?
