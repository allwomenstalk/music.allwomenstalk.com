# 7 Songs That Will Give You Strength to Change Your Life ...

Music is powerful, so it shouldn’t surprise you that there are songs that will give you strength to change your life. You can’t wait for things to turn around. You have to take charge of your own situation if you want to see results. For motivation, listen to these songs that will give you strength to change your life:

## 1. It’s My Life by Bon Jovi

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/music-video/its-my-life/306028214)
If you need to get pumped, this song will help get your adrenaline going. The beat is catchy and lyrics are inspirational: “I ain’t gonna live forever. I just want to live while I’m alive.” Bon Jovi knows what it feels like to take life by the reins. Follow his example, and “don’t bend, don’t break, baby don’t back down.” This is one of the songs that will give you strength to change your life. All you have to do is listen to it.

## 2. Jesus Take the Wheel by Carrie Underwood

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/f3bW1Z)
In this song, a mother almost crashes her car. It takes a near death experience for her to realize that she has to change her life. You don’t have to be like the woman in the song. You can vow to change your life before something tragic happens. Let this song be the inspiration you need to change for the better.

## 3. Live like You Were Dying by Tim McGraw

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/live-like-you-were-dying/68892833)
Live like you're dying has been a popular phrase for years, but not many have put it to the test. If you found out you didn’t have long to live, what would you do? Think about all of the things you want to do in life, and do them. What are you waiting for? This song lists all of the things that a man did after he received bad news from the doctors.

## 4. Change by Taylor Swift

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/90yguH)
Taylor Swift has a song about everything. This one talks about how anything is possible: “These walls that they put up to hold us back will fall down.” Even if you’re used to failure, it’s possible to succeed. All it takes is a little time and effort.

## 5. Not Afraid by Eminem

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/MRUaXV)
In this song, Eminem speaks about overcoming his drug addiction. He tells everyone that’s currently in a bad situation that they’ll be able to get out, just like he did. “Just letting you know that you’re not alone. Holla if you feel like you’ve been down the same road.” If you’re facing similar issues that he’s faced, then he could be your inspiration to overcome your addiction.

## 6. The Climb by Miley Cyrus

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/YTAKDu)
This is an older song by Miley that speaks about the importance of one’s journey. “It’s not about how fast I get there. It’s not about what’s waiting on the other side. It’s the climb.” Even if it feels like it’s taking you forever to get to the place you want to be, you have to remember how important the road you’re on is.

## 7. The Middle by Jimmy Eat World

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/jimmy-eat-world/3446996)
This song assures you that you‘re going to be okay. “Live right now. Just be yourself. It doesn’t matter if it’s good enough for someone else.” If you want an upbeat song that encourages you to do your best, then turn up this tune!

If you’ve never heard one of these songs, you might as well listen once or twice. What song do you listen to when you want to be motivated?
