# 7 Celebrities Who Have Praised Taylor Swifts New Album ...

Taylor Swift's new album "1989" was released at the end of last month and it's still making headlines. That's because she won't stop breaking records with it! Currently, she has the biggest album in the country with "1989" selling 1.2 million copies in its first week. That's more than ANY other album release this year! Her second week's figures shot as high as 400,000 copies sold and that alone (still) beats any album release this year. Crazy, right? A-list BFFs and celebrity fans were quick to praise Taylor Swift's new album:

## 1. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aa/vv/u3rd481h_600x788.jpg)

Selena and Taylor have been besties for years and are always vocal about their love for one another. When Taylor's album dropped, Selena posted an adorable collage of the two of them on her Instagram account! She captioned it, "From hotel room album listening parties, kitchen dance floor album listening parties to driving on the pch, windows down album listening parties. -The best part is we were the only ones there. Each time. So happy, honored and proud to know you and your diaries. @taylorswift #1989 BUY IT!!! -now I'll listen live from drowning in my bed sheets." Taylor replied to the post and told Selena that it absolutely made her day. Friendship goals, am I right? Selena's just one of many celebrities who've praised Taylor Swift's new album.

## 2. Lorde

![Lorde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/j1/krzltt1o_600x823.jpg)

Lorde has become very close to Taylor and the two are constantly posting cute photos of their hangouts. When "1989" came out, Lorde took to Twitter to let everyone know her thoughts! She said: "OMG 1989 IS OUT WHAT A DAY. so proud of my sista. my favourite is style (and also blank space) (and also all of them)" LOL, I can't pick ONE favorite either. She also added, "I first heard 'Style' driving down the pacific coast highway and it was EVERYTHING I chair-danced so hard you have no idea #TS1989."

## 3. Lena Dunham

![Lena Dunham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/zj/oqciuf7k_570x873.jpg)

Taylor worked with Lena's boyfriend Jack Antonoff for a lot of the songs on the album which means Lena definitely had an advantage as to knowing what was going on behind the scenes! Lucky her! Lena tweeted, "My favorite songs on #1989 are This Love, Blank Space, Out of the Woods and also every single song."

## 4. Ed Sheeran

![Ed Sheeran](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uv/p6/urjv372z_600x900.jpg)

Ed and Taylor are a friendship made in music heaven. The two are very supportive of each other which I think is SO cute! Taylor promoted Ed's "X" album when it came out and Ed did the same for 1989. He tweeted, "Go grab yourself @taylorswift13's new album, it just came out and it's stellar!"

## 5. Sarah Hyland

![Sarah Hyland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/of/fsblwodd_600x878.jpg)

Did you know that Taylor and Modern Family's Sarah Hyland are really close? The two even go to Broadway shows together! Sarah made sure to tweet out her support by quoting one of Taylor's lyrics: "Cuz you know I love the players/ and you love the game"- @taylorswift13. I love this woman." So do I!

## 6. Hayley Williams

![Hayley Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xo/i6/bcptdxlr_600x901.jpg)

Paramore's Hayley Williams couldn't get enough of Taylor's new album and I think that's awesome. She tweeted, "She done done it again! Tonight, my favorite is "This Love".. #TS1989" I can totally relate to having a new favorite song every night.

## 7. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/o5/ltybbqhj_600x813.jpg)

Hailee and Taylor bonded last year and became really close friends. When 1989 came out, Hailee tweeted: "I'm crying. It's so good. #1989." She also added, "So many unforgettable memories were made with RED. Looking forward to so many more in 1989. @taylorswift13"

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many celebrities who have praised the musical gift that is Taylor's new album! What do you think about 1989? Share your favorite songs in the comments!
