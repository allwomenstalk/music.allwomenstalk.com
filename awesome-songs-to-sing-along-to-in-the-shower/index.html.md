# 7 Insanely Awesome Songs for People Singing in the Shower ...

Everybody loves great songs to sing along to in the shower, but sometimes you get a little bored with singing the same songs over and over. These are some song suggestions to give you a different tune to enjoy. I’ve mixed some oldies in with contemporary hits to give you an eclectic mix.

## 1. “Life of the Party”

![“Life of the Party”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/a7/ea36uha855a54c8b850e8717386048_326x326.jpg)

‌On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/YB4EQY)﻿‌

This song by the fabulously popular Shawn Mendes is upbeat and will have you singing along in no time. It was his first single and became an instant chart topper. It’s still wildly popular and a great song to enjoy when you want to sing along. The lyrics are easy to remember, making it the perfect song to sing in the shower. It’s also a heartfelt song that you’ll feel the emotion in.

## 2. “Rockin' Robin”

![“Rockin' Robin”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/qn/v0vkd0oq55bda8ed01fef930261421_326x324.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/sd0OkF)

This is an oldie but a goodie. I think it’s an oldie that’s very loved but, for some reason, easy to forget about. It’s 55 years old so it’s very possible that your grandparents rocked along with robin, too! If you’ve never heard it then you need to. It’s one that’s sure to put you in a great mood for the day ahead.

## 3. “Déjà Vu ”

![“Déjà Vu ”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/j3/cl83gbe555bda8ee4b059981218845_326x297.jpg)

‌﻿‌On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/deja-vu-feat-jay-z-single/175554708)

Want a song that’ll get you pumped? This is where it’s at, ladies. Who doesn’t love Beyonce and Jay Z? “Déjà Vu” has an edgy sound that you’ll love singing along with. You may even find yourself using your shampoo bottle as a microphone!

## 4. ”Mama Said (There’d Be Days like This)”

![”Mama Said (There’d Be Days like This)”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/bq/hqic6uqh55bda8efbbdc2116405626_326x326.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/mama-said-thered-be-days-like/418556952)

Dreading your day ahead? This may be the song you need to face it. It’s got a realistic message you can identify with when you’re dealing with a tough time in your life. Yet the song doesn’t drag you down; it peps you up. Released by The Shirelles in 1961, it’s an oldie that’s as good now as it was then.

## 5. “Dear Future Husband”

![“Dear Future Husband”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/hb/ogh7phty55bda97030c29340557992_326x326.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/aaFBF9)

This recent hit by Meghan Trainor will make sure your day gets off to a fab start. It’s just fun and all about what Meghan expects in a future husband. It’s got a girl power feel that you’re going to love! The only problem with this song is that you’ll want to dance when you hear it, which can be dangerous in the shower! So sing and dance with caution!

## 6. “I Met Him on a Sunday”

![“I Met Him on a Sunday”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/wc/mp4xe5v255bda8efcf416519000958_326x326.jpg)

‌On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/25-all-time-greatest-hits/997503440)

This is another olden-golden hit by The Shirelles. Can you tell I’m slightly obsessed? I heard both this song and the other one mentioned in this article for the first time when I watched “Because I Said So,” which is a great chick flick if you haven’t seen it. This song is one you just can’t seem to get enough of. The lyrics are very easy to catch onto, making it a perfect song to sing along to in the shower. If you haven’t heard this one then you’re missing out!

## 7. “Honey I’m Good”

![“Honey I’m Good”](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/dl/kfu8bq3y55a54c8e8eb61659588765_326x326.jpg)

On iTunes at: [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/honey-im-good/891049725?i=891049733%3F)﻿‌

The first time I heard this song was on “Dancing with the Stars,” so I was a bit late on this one. However, I was instantly hooked! Andy Grammer is an amazing artist with a bright future. This song will make you feel good about your day ahead and give you positive vibes about your relationship, too. Who doesn’t love that?

These are 7 fantastic songs to sing along to in the shower. Now, it’s your turn to spill. What songs do you sing in the shower?
