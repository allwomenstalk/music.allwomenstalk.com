# Top 20 Girl Power Anthems All Ladies Need ...

Ready for some **girl power anthems**? Whether you are in your car getting ready to walk up to your boss for a raise, getting ready for a night out with your girls, getting over that good for nothing boy, or just trying to live your life as a woman, you need to have some girl anthems to get yourself moving and singing at the top of your lungs. Here are twenty of the top twenty **girl power anthems**. Cheers to a year of girl power and self-love.

## 1. Whole Lotta Woman

I’m a strong badass chick with classic confidence.

## 2. On My Own – Miley Cyrus

I came to this world by myself, so I don’t need anybody else.

## 3. Feelin’ Myself – Nicki Minaj (Feat. Beyonce)

Male or female, it makes no difference, I stop the world.

## 4. Prayin’ – Kesha

I’m proud of who I am. No more monsters, I can breathe again.

## 5. Confident – Demi Lovato

So leave the lights on. No, you can’t make me behave.

## 6. I’m Just a Girl – No Doubt

What I’ve succumbed to is making me numb.

## 7. Hard out Here – Lily Allen

And if you can’t detect the sarcasm, you’ve misunderstood.

## 8. F**kin’ Perfect – Pink

Done looking for the critics ‘cause they’re everywhere.

## 9. Run the World (Girls) -Beyonce

Endless power, with our love we can devour.

## 10. The Climb – Miley Cyrus

Ain’t about how fast I get there, ain’t about what’s waiting on the other side. It’s the climb.

## 11. Since You’ve Been Gone – Kelly Clarkson

Shut your mouth I just can’t take it.

## 12. Survivor – Destiny’s Child

You thought I would fail without you but I’m on top.

## 13. Man! I Feel like a Woman! – Shania Twain

The best thing about bein’ woman is the prerogative to have a little fun.

## 14. Girl on Fire – Aicia Keys

Yells: THIS GIRL IS ON FIRE.

## 15. Can’t Hold Us down – Christina Aguilera (Feat. Lil Kim)

Take a deep breath and say it loud. Never can, never will, can’t hold us down.

## 16. Independent Women, Part 1 – Destiny’s Child

Depend on no one else to give you what you want.

## 17. Wannabe – Sprice Girls

Make it last forever, friendship never ends.

## 18. No Scrubs – TLC

‘Cause I’m looking like class and he looking like trash.

## 19. Stronger – Britney Spears

I’m not your property as from today.

## 20. Fighter – Christina Aguilera

Cause if it wasn’t for all of your torture, I wouldn’t know how to be this way now.
