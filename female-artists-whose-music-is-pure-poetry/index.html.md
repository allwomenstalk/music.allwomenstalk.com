# 7 Female Artists Whose Music is Pure Poetry ...

All music is art, but sometimes an artist writes or sings a line that is pure poetry. So who are the **female artists whose music is pure poetry**?

These are the type of songs that you listen to as a writer and think, “I wish I had thought of that line!”

I’ve put together a list of a few female artists/bands with lead female vocalists whose songs are poetry, who string words together in beautiful lines that can speak to any woman’s soul. Here they are - the **female artists whose music is pure poetry**.

## 1. Adele

“When the world feels so cruel, and your heart makes you feel like a fool, I will be, I will be, your remedy.” - Remedy

## 2. Halestorm

“Put your lighter in the air and lead me back home.” - Break In

## 3. Halsey

“You were a vision in the morning when the light came through. I know I’ve only felt religion when I’ve lied with you.” - Colors

## 4. Icon for Hire

“Tryna make my misery just a piece of my history, a little less victim a little more victory.” - Happy Hurts

## 5. Zella Day

“Woven in the fabric of your tapestry, cover me in honeysuckle memories. Compass point you home, calling out from the east, compass points you anywhere, closer to me.” - Compass

## 6. Kesha

“You brought the flames and you put me through hell, I had to learn how to fight for myself, and we both know all the truth I could tell, I’ll just say this. I wish you farewell. I hope you’re somewhere praying, praying, I hope your soul is changing, changing.” - Praying

## 7. Little Mix

“Am I still not good enough? Am I still not worth that much? I’m sorry for the way my life turned out, sorry for the smile I’m wearing now. Guess I’m still not good enough.” - Good Enough
