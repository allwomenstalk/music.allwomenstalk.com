# 7 Covers by Lennon and Maisy Stella That'll Amaze You ...

There’s no way anyone wouldn’t fall in love with these adorable girls after watching covers by Lennon and Maisy Stella. The duo are incredibly talented singers/actresses. How they manage to be SO talented and successful at such a young age is beyond me! But, I’m glad these young girls have remained sweethearts. I have a feeling they’ll go very far in the music industry. In case you didn’t know, the two star as the Conrad sisters in ABC’s “Nashville”. Check out these covers by Lennon and Maisy Stella and decide for yourself if they’re as talented as I believe they are!

## 1. “Call Your Girlfriend”

This is one of my absolute favorite covers by Lennon and Maisy Stella! It’s really the one that put the two on everyone’s radar. The video was uploaded back in 2012 and went viral. It’s nearing 22 million views already! The two had huge stars tweeting about them and spreading the word. Aren’t they great?

## 2. “Headlock”

This video gives me chills. It just doesn’t make sense how good they are! This video was also uploaded back in ’12 and is nearing 4 million views. Their vocals are shown off perfectly with this song. I love their harmonies! Joanna “Jojo” Levesque and Harry Styles from One Direction both tweeted about how impressed they were by this video!

## 3. "I Won’t Give up”

How amazing does this sound? The two took on a Jason Mraz song and let me tell you, they did it justice. The video is already nearing 8.5 million views! Lennon’s guitar skills and Maisy’s harmonies are to die for. They are just the sweetest thing! They even inspired a bunch of YouTube covers where people cover this song the “Lennon and Maisy version”.

## 4. “Ho Hey”

This is a clip from a Nashville episode and it’s very impressive. The two play the daughters of a famous country star, and aspire to go into music as well. Well, they’d definitely succeed if they tried! Lennon and Maisy even got to perform this at the Grand Ole Opry. How many people can say they did that?

## 5. “You Belong with Me”

This video is too adorable! The girls performed Taylor’s song at the CMA Awards right before she was awarded the Pinnacle Award. How cute was that hug between Taylor and Maisy after the song? I loved their rendition of the song. It’s just as catchy as the pop version!

## 6. “Telescope”

Here’s another clip from Nashville! The Conrad sisters perform at a talent show and sing the song of another famous country star, Juliette Barnes. Once again, their harmonies are everything. I’d feel sorry for whoever had to follow their act at the talent show!

## 7. “Ring of Fire”

To celebrate Johnny Cash’s birthday, the Stella sisters performed “Ring of Fire” at The Ryman Auditorium in Nashville. I’m a big fan of this cover because I’m a big Cash fan! This song is so beautiful and I think the girls really did it justice. Maisy’s high notes give me the chills! Lennon has this awesome smooth voice. Together, they’re just amazing.

There you have it! There’s so many incredible videos of these two girls singing so if you want to see more, make sure to check them out on YouTube! Which was your favorite cover?
