# 7 Touching Songs about Trying to Make Your Relationship Work ...

Every relationship has rough patches, but only some couples are strong enough to get through them. Don't freak out if you're currently in a sticky situation with your boyfriend, because if he's the right one for you, you'll be able to find a way to make things work. Sure, it'll be hard, but isn't he worth the struggle? Here are some touching songs about trying to make your relationship work that'll reassure you things can change:

## 1. Just Give Me a Reason by P!nk and Nate Ruess

You've probably heard this song before, because it was pretty popular a year ago. It's about a couple that is hoping their relationship is just bent, and not completely broken. They both know that they don't want to give up on each other, so they're looking for a sign that things will be okay in the end. Not only are the lyrics touching, but P!nk and Nate's voices are absolutely stunning. The song is bound to give you chills.

## 2. It’s Not over by Secondhand Serenade

If you're on the verge of a breakup, you'll be able to relate to this song. Throughout it, the singer talks about how his relationship is over. However, at the end, he changes the lyrics to say that it's not actually over. He just won't give up.

## 3. Strong Enough to Bend by Tanya Tucker

This country song is pretty upbeat, because it has a positive message behind it. It compares a woman's relationship to a tree that always bends in the wind, but never ends up breaking. She knows that her relationship will last if her and her man can act as strong as that tree.

## 4. We Need a Resolution by Aaliyah

How long has it been since you've heard Aaliyah? This song is about her knowing that she can solve her problems with her man. Even though they both have issues and are both hurt, they miss each other and need to come to a resolution. She won't rest until that happens.

## 5. Whatever It Takes by Lifehouse

Here's another song that you've probably heard before. It's time to pay close attention to the lyrics, because it talks about how the singer will do whatever he can to make things right with his girlfriend. If she decides to stay, then he'll finally treat her the way that she deserves.

## 6. All the Same by Sick Puppies

If you've never heard of this band, you should check out their albums, because every track is just as great as this one. This song in particular is about a guy who doesn't care about his girlfriend's flaws. He just wants her to be there beside him.

## 7. Ain’t Too Proud to Beg by the Temptations

This is an old song, but it's just as great as anything on the radio today. It's about a man who refuses to let his girlfriend go. He'll get on his knees and beg her to stay if he has to, because he needs her in his life. It's pretty romantic when you think about it.

Don't waste your time crying when you could spend time trying to make things right with your man. If you two are meant to be, you'll be able to make things work. What other songs do you know with a similar theme?
