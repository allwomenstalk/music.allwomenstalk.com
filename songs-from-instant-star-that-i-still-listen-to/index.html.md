# 7 Songs from Instant Star That I Still Listen to ...

Please tell me I'm not the only one who still listens to songs from Instant Star. It was probably my all-time favorite TV show. It aired on The N (which is now known as Teen Nick) and I was obsessed with it. It revolved around a young singer/songwriter who won a talent competition and just like that, was catapulted into superstardom. Each week, a new song was featured and it was just amazing. The lyrics in these songs are beautiful, I still can't get enough of these songs from Instant Star!

## 1. Skin

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/skin/161191655?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=161191892)

I think this song will always be relatable to people. It's a song of pure heartache and betrayal. We've all been there, and Jude Harrison sings what we're all thinking. We all have that one person who just manages to get under our skin with all their lies, and this song will help you work through that. I honestly think this is such a beautiful song. It's one of my favorite songs from Instant Star.

## 2. I Don't Know if I Should Stay

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/i-dont-know-if-i-should-stay/389511570?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=389511715)

Close your eyes and just listen to this song. I promise you that it'll take you to another place. It's about being in a situation where you don't know what's best, staying or going. That kind of situation is always tough. This song talks about exactly what you're thinking during those moments. I love it so much!

## 3. Your Eyes

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/your-eyes/59562676?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=59562825)

This is just a beautiful love song. It's about getting lost in someone else. After all, the eyes are the window to the soul, right? You can just stare into someone's eyes and know everything you need to know. The lyrics include, "Your eyes are holding up the sky, your eyes make make me scared to tell the truth- I thought my heart was bulletproof."

## 4. Pick up the Pieces

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/pick-up-the-pieces/59562676?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=59562813)

This is definitely one of my favorite songs from the show. I think the lyrics are so beautiful! By the way, that high note that Alexz Johnson hits?! Wow. That girl has some serious pipes on her. So impressive.

## 5. Let Me Fall

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/let-me-fall/59562676?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=59562730)

I love this song because I love all the high notes that she hits in it. It has such a beautiful melody but still has great lyrics. It's about wanting to prove to someone that you have the strength to get up no matter times they shove you down. The lyrics include, "I want to lose control, I'm not afraid to lose it all. 'Cause I will get up again, if you let me fall." So powerful.

## 6. It Could Be You

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/it-could-be-you/161191655?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=161192039)

I love this song because it has more of rock edge than all of her other stuff. I remember this song and the episode that went along with it. It was one of my favorites! It's about the idea of a new relationship and the lyrics include, "Show me who you are, stop me before I go too far."

## 7. There's Us

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/theres-us/137770876?amp%3Bat=11l4BD&i=137770890)

This is such a beautiful ballad. It's about reminiscing about all the good times in a relationship, all the memories that you don't want to forget. My favorite lyric from the song goes, "Not everything is supposed to come true, some words are best unsaid, some love is not really love at all."

Well, there you have it! Those are just seven out of many songs from Instant Star that I still listen to. To be honest, I don't think I'll ever get tired of them. Which song was your favorite?
