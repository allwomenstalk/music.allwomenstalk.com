# Moving Songs to Help You Deal with Divorce in the Family ...

If your parents are separating, there are songs to help you deal with divorce. You're not alone, so you shouldn't feel like no one else will understand how you're feeling. There are plenty of people who have dealt with similar situations. If you're having trouble coping, here are some songs to help you deal with divorce:

## 1. Family Portrait by P!nk

This is the perfect song for anyone who has had to listen to their parents screaming and throwing things while they sit in their room crying. The child in the song desperately wants her family to remain whole and voices all of the worries that any child of divorce will have. It's one of the best songs to help you deal with divorce, because it will show you that you're not alone. There are hundreds of other families that look happy, but actually have issues. Don't think that you're the only one in your situation, because you're not.

## 2. Stay Together for the Kids by Blink-182

The video begins by stating that 50 percent of American households are destroyed by divorce. The song goes on to talk about how some parents will stay together for their kids, which is more dangerous than divorce. It's horrible to know that your parents are unhappy with their marriage, but don't they deserve to find happiness somewhere else? Try not to blame them for doing what they need to do, even if you don't understand their feelings.

## 3. Wonderful by Everclear

Even though divorce can be a messy process, everything will be okay again one day. Don't let yourself believe that your life is always going to be horrible, just because your parents are separated. It's not the end of the world, even if it feels like it is. Whether you believe it or not, everything will be wonderful again one day.

## 4. Mockingbird by Eminem

Eminem isn't always crude--he can also be extremely sentimental. This song is for his daughter, explaining how much her mother and him love her. Even though they aren't going to be in a relationship ever again, he still loves his daughter and doesn't want her to think otherwise. This song will remind you that your parents will always care about you and want what's best for you. They didn't mean to hurt you.

## 5. I Don't Love You by My Chemical Romance

If you want a song to cry to, this is the one. It's about a man and woman who once loved each other, but end up losing their feelings for each other. It can be difficult to listen to, so make sure that you can handle it when you press play. It might help you see the divorce from your parents' point of view.

## 6. Highway 20 Ride by Zach Brown Band

This song is about a man trying to explain his divorce to his child. He feels bad about what he had to do, but it was necessary. He just wants his son to know that he has always cared about him, and always will, despite what happened between him and his mother.

## 7. Someday Never Comes by Creedence Clearwater Revival

Here's one last song to help you deal with divorce. Your parents are probably going to tell you that you'll understand their situation someday, but that might not be true. But even if you never understand it, you can always accept it. It'll just take time.

Divorce isn't the worst thing in the world, so you can't let it ruin your life. It'll be hard to accept at first, but you'll feel better one day. How did you deal with your parents divorce?
