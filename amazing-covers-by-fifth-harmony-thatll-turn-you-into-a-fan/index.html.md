# 7 Amazing Covers by Fifth Harmony That'll Turn You into a Fan ...

They came in third place on X Factor last season, but these covers by Fifth Harmony will have you thinking that they should’ve taken the first prize home. The group is made up of five incredibly talented girls. Individually, they each have such powerful vocals. Together? They’re pretty much unstoppable. These are the times where you have to stop and thank Simon Cowell for being quite the genius. Check out these amazing covers by Fifth Harmony, they're sure to turn you into a fan!

## 1. Honeymoon Avenue

This rendition is everything. I actually hadn’t heard the original (sung by Ariana Grande) until after I heard this cover. Each of the girls showcases her vocals beautifully. It’s tough to choose a favorite part but I think I have to go with Camila’s solo (don't kill me harmonizers, I love them all!). Ariana Grande even proclaimed her love for the cover on Twitter! I absolutely love this song now and it tops my list for amazing covers by Fifth Harmony.

## 2. Wannabe

Okay, how cute is this?! I loved that the girls each dressed up as a Spice Girl. I was kind of worried that a cover of “Wannabe” might end up being super cheesy, but it wasn’t! They made it really fun and modern and I loved it. Mel B, a former Spice Girl herself, even tweeted the girls about their performance! She said was impressed and loved the girl power from the band.

## 3. Red

There’s tons of Taylor Swift covers out on YouTube. However, I think this one really stands out. The girls each have a solo part that’s unbelievably good. The high notes are so fantastic! I really wish Swift sees the cover, I’m dying to know what she’d think of it! Come to think of it, 5H would be an awesome opening act for Taylor.

## 4. Lego House

This was the cover that made me love Fifth Harmony. There’s just no disputing their talent in it! In my opinion, you know you’re good when Ed Sheeran gives you his approval. That’s exactly what happened when Ed saw their cover of his song! It’s such an awesome rendition and I love listening to it.

## 5. Stay

“Stay” was my song when it came out! I love it because theres so much power behind the song and the lyrics. The girls did an awesome job at portraying that emotion. I loved the beginning of it. I love their acoustic versions!

## 6. Mirrors (with Boyce Avenue)

This video got over 31 million views. Isn’t that crazy?! The girls got together with Boyce Avenue to do this duet and it really was the most perfect pairing. They all compliment each others voices so well. Not to mention, this is a great Justin Timberlake song. What’s not to love?

## 7. Royals

The girls started singing this song when they went on tour! I saw them perform this song in NYC (when they opened up for Cher Lloyd). They sounded great, as usual. I love how they always find ways to put a twist on a popular song and make it sound like it's their own.

That sums up my list for amazing covers by Fifth Harmony. The girls have done a lot more and I’m sure will do even more in the future. If you weren’t already, are you a fan now?
