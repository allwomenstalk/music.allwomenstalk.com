# 9 Upbeat Songs That'll Get You in the Flirting Mood ...

When you’re ready to go out on the town, you should prepare by listening to some upbeat songs. Music can put you in the mood for anything. If you’re trying to get a guy’s attention, you might as well listen to some upbeat songs that will make you feel flirty. The next time you’re ready to seduce someone, listen to one of these:

## 1. Are You Gonna Be My Girl by Jet

This is one of the upbeat songs that’s impossible not to dance along to. If you haven’t heard it yet, you’re missing out. Jet is one of the greatest bands of all time, and their songs are guaranteed to get you pumped.

## 2. Alright with Me by Kris Allen

This song is perfect for anyone with a crush. Have you ever stared at a cutie from across the room, and knew that he was glancing back at you, too? This talks about how much fun it can be to flirt, especially when you know the other person is just as interested as you are.

## 3. Can’t Keep My Hands off You by Simple Plan

Simple Plan’s old music deals with pain and heartache, but their newest album is more fun and lively. This song speaks about how obsessed people in relationships can get. When you’re in love, all you want to do is be with that person. You neglect everything else in your life, because you just want to have fun with your partner.

## 4. Hung up by Hot Chelle Ray

This song says everything you wish a guy would say to you. The lead singer talks about being so crazy about his girl that he doesn't want anyone else. If you want to be someone's one and only, you'll love these lyrics.

## 5. I Want You by Kelly Clarkson

This is a sweet song by an even sweeter singer. You know how unbelievably talented Kelly is by now, so there's no reason to skip over this song. It's about the 'perfect' guy that Kelly just can't get enough of. If you currently have a crush, you'll definitely relate.

## 6. Hot Stuff by Ashlee Simpson

The tune will make you dance and the lyrics will make you laugh. Ashlee sings about walking into a club, and seducing the men there. She claims that she doesn’t need to wear a short skirt and provocative clothing in order to score a hottie.

## 7. I Wanna by the All American Rejects

Do you daydream about kissing a certain cutie? Listen to Tyson Ritter sing about how badly he wants to touch his girl. If it doesn't get you ready for a make-out session, what will?

## 8. Friday I’m in Love by the Cure

This song talks about liking your crush at the end of the day, even if you occassionally get angry at them. You've done that before, haven't you? You think you're over someone, but you eventually realize that you're still in love with them.

## 9. You, You, You by Julianne Hough

Julianne can do more than just dance. This country song talks about how far she would go to be with the love of her life. It’s a sweet song that you have to hear at least once.

If these songs have successfully gotten you into a flirty mood, get out there and have some fun! What songs do you listen to before going out for the night?
