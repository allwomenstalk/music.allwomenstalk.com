# Hottest Songs You Should Be Listening to Right Now ...

There are a lot of great songs out right now, so I thought I would share some of my favorites with you. Some are more well known than others, but all have one thing in common : they must be listened to!

‌

## 1. Shape of You – Ed Sheeran

We’ve been waiting for new music from Ed. This is great.

## 2. Drinkin Too Much –Sam Hunt

Sam is great, and the end of this song in particular gets to me!

## 3. Name for You- the Shins

This song is so catchy!

## 4. On Hold – the Xx

We’re obsessed with The xx, end of story.

## 5. Distraction – Kehlani

It’s hard not to sing along, and Kehlani is fierce!

## 6. Welcome to Your Life – Grouplove

Turn it up in your car. You won’t regret it.

## 7. Caroline – Aminé

Impossible not to crank this one up.

## 8. Sidewalks – the Weeknd

Our personal favorite from the recent album.

## 9. I Don’t Wanna Live Forever –Taylor Swift Ft. Zayn

We’re not the biggest T. Swift fans, but this song is addicting.

## 10. Make Me (CRY) - Noah Cyrus Ft. Labrinth

This combo of voices is one we love. And Miley’s little sister? She’s great.

## 11. Cold Water –Major Lazer Ft. Justin Bieber, MØ

A great DJ plus Justin? We love it.

## 12. Tear in My Heart – Twenty One Pilots Ft. Mutemath

Twenty One Pilots did some remixes with Mutemath, and they are killer!

## 13. False Alarm – the Head and the Heart

We love this easygoing, airy song from their latest album!

## 14. Reverend – Kings of Leon

Kings of Leon have such a distinct sound, and we love them for it!

## 15. Capsize – Frenship & Emily Warren

The lyrics in this song are so relatable, and the music video is stunning!

## 16. Somebody else – the 1975

So we’ve heard this one a lot on the radio. But it’s so good.

## 17. City of Stars – Ryan Gosling & Emma Stone

La La Land is one of the biggest movies right now, and we love these two singing together.

## 18. Don’t Wanna Know – Maroon 5 Ft. Kendrick Lamar

The beat to this song is too catchy!

## 19. Better – OneRepublic

OneRepublic always has quality music, and this album brought no less!

## 20. Go Robot – Red Hot Chili Peppers

We love everything from Red Hot Chili Peppers. The end.

## 21. Send My Love (to Your New Lover) – Adele

Adele is queen. And looks amazing in this video.
