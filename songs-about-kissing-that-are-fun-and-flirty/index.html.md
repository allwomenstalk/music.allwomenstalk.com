# 7 Songs about Kissing That Are Fun and Flirty ...

When you're lost in a daydream about your crush or anxious to see your partner, you can listen to songs about kissing. They'll make you crave your man (or woman) even more than you already do. If you're lucky enough to be sitting right next to your partner while you play these songs, then give them a few kisses while you listen to the tunes. Here are some catchy songs about kissing that you'll want to play again and again:

## 1. Hands All over by Maroon 5

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/qrM9MQ)

You know Maroon 5, but you may not know this song. It's about wanting someone else to put their hands on you. If you don't have anyone in mind while listening, then Adam Levine will make you pine for him. He looks gorgeous in the music video--but when doesn't he? This is one of the catchy songs about kissing you'll be singing the rest of the day.

## 2. Kiss You by One Direction

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/take-me-home/572141438)

You have to admit that these boys have some serious talent. All of their songs are catchy, and make you want to dance. Not all men are nice, but these guys will make you believe that there's good in people. They're so sweet, and would treat any girl like a princess.

## 3. Kiss Me by New Found Glory

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/from-screen-to-your-stereo/274929520)

This is a cover of a song by Sixpence None the Richer. They sing it as wonderfully as New Found Glory does, so you should check it out once you're finished listening to this version. It'll be worth it.

## 4. I Wanna by All American Rejects

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/music-video/i-wanna/336272580)

This song talks about how bad the urge can be to kiss someone else. However you're doing in the love department, you can probably relate to the lyrics. If the song doesn't entertain you, then the music video should. The very beginning of it has "Gives You Hell" playing in the background of the party. It's a cute little nod to their other songs on the album.

## 5. Just a Kiss by Lady Antebellum

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/music-video/just-a-kiss/719802712)

This song talks about taking it slow. When you meet someone new, you don't have to take too many steps at once. Sometimes, just a kiss is all you need. It's a sweet gesture that can lead to love.

## 6. Untouched by the Veronicas

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/hook-me-up/279690686)

This song should get you moving to the beat. It's difficult to listen to it once and only once. You'll want to keep playing it, because it's so darn catchy. Like the rest of the songs on the list, this one talks about how hard it is to resist the one that you love. They can drive you crazy, in the best way possible.

## 7. First Kiss by 3oh!3 and Kesha

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/my-first-kiss-remix-feat/384246242)

Do you remember your first kiss? If you didn't have it yet, then don't worry. It'll happen eventually, and it's different for everybody. This song talks about how much fun it can be, and will get you excited to meet new people. You never know when you're going to run into your knight in shining armor. He could be right around the corner, hoping to meet you, too.

These songs can help you get through your alone time. For now, you're not with the one that you love, but you will be eventually. Was your first kiss amazing or absolutely horrible?
